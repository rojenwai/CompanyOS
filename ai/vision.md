# AI Engineering - Vision

- Horizon 1 - No model or prompt ships without passing an evaluation suite.
- Horizon 2 - Every AI feature is grounded, versioned, observable, and cost-tracked by default.
- Horizon 3 - Systems that evaluate and improve themselves within guardrails, with humans on the high-risk loop.

The end state: AI is the company's most dependable engineering surface, not its riskiest.
