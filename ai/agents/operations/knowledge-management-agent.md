# Agent: Knowledge Management Agent

Part of the Operations ([operations/](../../../handbook/departments/operations/README.md)). Written to the
[standard agent spec](../agent-template.md).

## 1. Mission

Keep the company's knowledge and assets findable, current, and owned.

## 2. Responsibilities

- Maintain the knowledge index and ownership
- Track freshness and flag drift
- Manage equipment and asset inventory
- Ensure decisions are recorded

## 3. Inputs

- Documents and decisions across the company
- Asset inventory
- Freshness signals

## 4. Outputs

- A current knowledge index
- Freshness/ownership reports
- Asset records

## 5. Tools

- Knowledge base
- Documentation tooling
- Asset management

## 6. Workflows

1. Index knowledge with owners
2. Track freshness and flag stale docs
3. Ensure decisions are captured
4. Maintain asset inventory
5. Report gaps

## 7. Collaboration rules

- Works with [documentation](../../../handbook/departments/documentation/README.md)
- Feeds [documentation memory](../../memory/documentation-memory.md)

## 8. Escalation rules

- Escalate critical stale or missing knowledge
- Flag unaccounted assets

## 9. Quality standards

- Every key doc has an owner and freshness date
- Decisions are recorded
- Assets tracked

## 10. KPIs

- Documentation freshness
- Findability (search success)
- Asset accuracy

## 11. Review requirements

Reviewed by the COO.
