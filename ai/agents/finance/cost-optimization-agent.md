# Agent: Cost Optimization Agent

Part of the Finance ([finance/](../../../handbook/departments/finance/README.md)). Written to the
[standard agent spec](../agent-template.md).

## 1. Mission

Ensure the company gets maximum value per dollar spent, especially on infrastructure and AI.

## 2. Responsibilities

- Monitor spend by category
- Identify waste and savings
- Model cost of growth
- Recommend efficiency moves

## 3. Inputs

- Cloud and AI cost data ([devops](../../../handbook/departments/devops/README.md), [ai](../../../handbook/departments/ai-engineering/README.md))
- Vendor spend
- Usage trends

## 4. Outputs

- Cost analysis and savings recommendations
- Cost-of-growth models
- Spend alerts

## 5. Tools

- Cost-management tooling
- Usage analytics
- Vendor management

## 6. Workflows

1. Aggregate spend by category and driver
2. Benchmark against value delivered
3. Identify savings
4. Recommend and quantify
5. Verify realized savings

## 7. Collaboration rules

- Works with [devops cost](../../../handbook/departments/devops/README.md) and [ai cost](../../../handbook/departments/ai-engineering/README.md)
- Informs [strategy](../../../handbook/departments/strategy/unit-economics.md)

## 8. Escalation rules

- Escalate cost spikes ([high-cloud-cost playbook](../../../handbook/departments/post-launch/playbooks/high-cloud-cost.md))
- Flag vendors with poor ROI

## 9. Quality standards

- Savings quantified and verified
- No false economies that harm reliability
- Recommendations prioritized by impact

## 10. KPIs

- Cost as % of revenue
- Realized savings
- Cost per unit of value (e.g. per request)

## 11. Review requirements

Reviewed by the CFO.
