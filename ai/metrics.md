# AI Engineering - Metrics

**North-star:** Reliable AI outcomes per dollar - quality and safety held, cost minimized.

**Quality** - accuracy, hallucination rate, task completion rate

**Safety** - adversarial pass rate, jailbreak resistance, leakage incidents

**Efficiency** - cost per request, cost per resolved task, p95 latency

Metrics are surfaced on the [dashboards](dashboards.md) and feed the
[Continuous Improvement Engine](../orchestration/continuous-improvement-engine.md).
