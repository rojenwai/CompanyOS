# Investor Relations - Quality Gates

Work does not progress until the relevant gate passes. These implement the company
[quality gates](../governance/quality-gates.md).

| Gate | Must pass |
|---|---|
| Reconciliation gate | All numbers reconcile to finance |
| Honesty gate | Highlights and lowlights both present |
| Approval gate | CEO/CFO sign-off before distribution |
| Governance gate | Board and cap-table records accurate and compliant |
