# Investor Relations - Metrics

**North-star:** Durable investor trust - reflected in easy diligence and faster raises.

**Communication** - update cadence adherence, investor engagement, reconciliation accuracy

**Governance** - materials-on-time rate, action-item closure, governance compliance

**Readiness** - data-room freshness, cap-table accuracy, filing timeliness

Metrics are surfaced on the [dashboards](dashboards.md) and feed the
[Continuous Improvement Engine](../orchestration/continuous-improvement-engine.md).
