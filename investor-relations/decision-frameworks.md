# Investor Relations - Decision Frameworks

How decisions are made here. All decisions follow the company
[decision framework](../governance/decision-framework.md); these are the department-specific ones.

- **Disclose now vs. wait** - Disclose material information promptly and honestly; never sit on bad news.
- **Level of detail** - Enough for investors to trust and act; consistent period to period.
- **Raise timing** - Coordinate with [strategy](../strategy/unit-economics.md) and finance on runway and terms.

Reversible decisions are made fast and revisited; irreversible ones require review and are recorded in
[decision memory](../memory/decision-memory.md).
