# Investor Relations - Standards

Testable standards, not aspirations. These extend the company-wide
[standards](../standards/README.md).

1. Every reported number reconciles exactly to finance.
2. Updates follow a consistent, reliable cadence.
3. Bad news is reported early and honestly.
4. Facts and projections are clearly separated.
5. Board and cap-table records are accurate, timely, and compliant.
