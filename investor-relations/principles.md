# Investor Relations - Principles

Non-negotiable. Every piece of work is judged against these.

1. **Report reality, early.** Never hide or delay bad news.
2. **Numbers reconcile.** IR metrics match [finance](../finance/README.md) exactly.
3. **No surprises to the board.** Material changes are communicated proactively.
4. **Consistent cadence.** Investors know when and what to expect.
5. **Confidentiality and compliance.** Handle material information correctly.
6. **Separate facts from projections** in every communication.
