## Rules

- Objectives are qualitative and few (2-4). Key Results are measurable and honest.
- Every KR maps to a real [metric](../../standards/README.md) with an owner - no un-instrumented KRs.
- Score at quarter end (0.0-1.0); 0.7 is a healthy stretch, 1.0 every time means aim higher.
- Mid-quarter, KRs are tracked, not silently changed. Learnings feed the next cycle via the
  [Continuous Improvement Engine](../../../ai/orchestration/continuous-improvement-engine.md).
