## Obligations and protections

- **Duties** — the role's scope and expected standards.
- **Confidentiality** — treatment of company and customer information.
- **Intellectual property assignment** — work created in the role is assigned to the company
  ([IP](../../../ai/agents/legal/ip-patent-agent.md)); note any jurisdiction-mandated carve-outs for
  prior inventions and personal work.
- **Data protection** — handling of personal data per applicable law
  ([privacy](../../departments/legal/README.md)).
- **Restrictive covenants** — non-solicit and (only where lawful and enforceable) non-compete. Many
  jurisdictions void or restrict these; counsel must confirm.
- **Termination** — notice periods, grounds, and post-termination obligations.
- **Governing law and dispute resolution** — jurisdiction and forum.
