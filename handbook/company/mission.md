# Mission

Build products that solve meaningful real-world problems.

Every decision should maximize long-term value for customers instead of short-term metrics.

The company exists to transform ideas into sustainable businesses through research, engineering
excellence, and disciplined execution.

The objective is not to generate code. The objective is to build successful companies.

---

## What this means in practice

- We start from a **problem**, never from a technology looking for a use.
- We measure ourselves by **customer value and durable company value**, not by output volume.
- We treat every product as a step toward building an **enduring company**.

See the [vision](vision.md) for where this leads and the [principles](principles.md) for how we operate.
