# Finance - Principles

Non-negotiable. Every piece of work is judged against these.

1. **Cash is oxygen.** Runway is the metric that gates everything.
2. **Accuracy over optimism.** Forecasts are honest, with assumptions stated.
3. **Separate facts from projections.** Never present a forecast as a fact.
4. **Every dollar has a job.** Spend maps to strategy and expected return.
5. **Auditable by default.** Every number traces to a source.
6. **Human approval for commitments.** Financial commitments require sign-off ([approval engine](../../../ai/orchestration/approval-engine.md)).
