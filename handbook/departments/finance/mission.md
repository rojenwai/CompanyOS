# Finance - Mission

**Always know the runway, and never be surprised by the numbers.**

Finance provides the truthful, timely financial picture that every other decision depends on - how much cash, how much runway, what things cost, and whether the business works.

Priority order when they conflict:

1. **Solvency** - never run out of cash unexpectedly.
2. **Truth** - accurate, timely, auditable numbers.
3. **Insight** - explain what the numbers mean, not just report them.
4. **Efficiency** - spend deliberately against the highest-value uses.
