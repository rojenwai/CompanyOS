# Finance - Metrics

**North-star:** Sustainable runway with efficient, high-return spend.

**Liquidity** - cash, runway months, net burn

**Performance** - revenue, gross margin, budget variance

**Efficiency** - cost as % of revenue, cost per unit of value, DSO

Metrics are surfaced on the [dashboards](dashboards.md) and feed the
[Continuous Improvement Engine](../../../ai/orchestration/continuous-improvement-engine.md).
