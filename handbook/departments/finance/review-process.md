# Finance - Review Process

Financial outputs are reviewed for accuracy (do they reconcile?), transparency (are assumptions stated?), and control (were approvals obtained?). Material variances and any commitment above threshold require CFO review.

Reviews are independent - the [Reviewer](../../../ai/orchestration/reviewer.md) never assumes the author is
correct. Security-sensitive work also requires the
[Security Reviewer](../../../ai/orchestration/security-reviewer.md).
