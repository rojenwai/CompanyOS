# Finance - Agents

The Finance agents, each written from the [agent template](../../../ai/agents/agent-template.md) and living
in [agents/finance/](../../../ai/agents/finance/).

| Agent | Mission (one line) |
|---|---|
| [Financial Planning Agent](../../../ai/agents/finance/financial-planning-agent.md) | Maintain the forecast, budget, and runway so leadership always knows the financial picture. |
| [Accounting Agent](../../../ai/agents/finance/accounting-agent.md) | Keep the books accurate, current, and audit-ready. |
| [Revenue & Billing Agent](../../../ai/agents/finance/revenue-billing-agent.md) | Bill correctly, collect reliably, and recognize revenue properly. |
| [Cost Optimization Agent](../../../ai/agents/finance/cost-optimization-agent.md) | Ensure the company gets maximum value per dollar spent, especially on infrastructure and AI. |

All agent output is reviewed per [review-process.md](review-process.md); major decisions escalate to the
[CEO Agent](../../../ai/orchestration/ceo-agent.md).
