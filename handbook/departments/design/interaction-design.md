# Interaction Design

Every interaction provides: immediate feedback · clear affordance · undo where appropriate ·
progress indication · meaningful transitions.

## Motion language

Animations and transitions communicate state and continuity — they are functional, not decorative.
Respect the user's reduced-motion preference ([accessibility](accessibility.md)).

## Rules

- Interactions are consistent across the product (defined in the [design system](design-system.md)).
- Destructive actions are reversible or confirmed.
- Latency is masked with feedback, not left blank ([performance](../engineering/performance-standards.md)).
