# Design - Mission

**Make the product effortless to understand and use, for everyone.**

A feature that works but confuses is a failed feature. Design's job is to remove friction between the
customer's intent and the outcome they want - through clear structure, honest feedback, consistent
patterns, and accessibility by default.

Priority order when they conflict:

1. **Accessible** - everyone can use it ([accessibility.md](accessibility.md)).
2. **Clear** - the user always knows where they are and what to do next.
3. **Consistent** - the same thing works the same way everywhere ([design-system.md](design-system.md)).
4. **Efficient** - the common task takes the fewest steps.
5. **Delightful** - polish and motion that serve the task, never distract from it.

Design serves the [product mission](../product/mission.md): the product exists to solve customer
problems, and design is how that solution is experienced.
