# Design - Principles

Non-negotiable. Every screen and interaction is judged against these.

1. **Accessibility is a requirement, not a feature.** Meet [WCAG](accessibility.md) - keyboard, screen
   reader, contrast, scalable text, reduced motion. If it is not accessible, it is not done.
2. **Clarity over cleverness.** The obvious design beats the impressive one. Reduce cognitive load.
3. **Consistency.** The same action looks and behaves the same everywhere - enforced by the
   [design system](design-system.md). One-off patterns are debt.
4. **Feedback for every action.** Every interaction confirms it was received - immediately
   ([interaction-design.md](interaction-design.md)).
5. **Visual hierarchy.** The most important thing is the most prominent thing.
6. **Forgiveness.** Prevent errors; make them recoverable; support undo.
7. **Honesty.** The interface never implies capabilities the product lacks; loading and empty states are
   designed, not afterthoughts.
8. **Evidence over taste.** Validate with [users](usability-testing.md); "I think it looks better" is not
   a reason.
9. **Motion serves function.** Animation communicates state and continuity - never decoration.
