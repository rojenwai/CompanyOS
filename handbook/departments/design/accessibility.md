# Accessibility

Meet WCAG standards. Accessibility is a default requirement, not a later enhancement.

## Support

Keyboard navigation · screen readers · high contrast · scalable fonts · color blindness ·
reduced motion · accessible forms.

## Rules

- Every [design-system](design-system.md) component is accessible out of the box.
- [Frontend](../../../ai/agents/engineering/frontend-engineer.md) verifies keyboard, screen-reader, and
  contrast behavior before merge ([review process](../engineering/review-process.md)).
- Accessibility is checked in [usability testing](usability-testing.md) and gated at launch
  ([product launch checklist](../product/quality-gates.md)).
