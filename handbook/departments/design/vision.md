# Design - Vision

- **Horizon 1 - Coherent.** One design system, adopted everywhere; every surface accessible by default;
  no inconsistent one-off UI.
- **Horizon 2 - Evidence-driven.** Every significant design decision is backed by
  [research](ux-research.md) and validated by [usability testing](usability-testing.md), not opinion.
- **Horizon 3 - Adaptive.** Interfaces that adapt to context, ability, and device, and a design system
  that partly maintains itself - tokens and components flowing straight into code.

The end state: customers never think about the interface, because it never gets in their way.
