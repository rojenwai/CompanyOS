# People & HR - Standards

Testable standards, not aspirations. These extend the company-wide
[standards](../../standards/README.md).

1. Every role has a scorecard; every hire is evaluated against it.
2. Interviews are structured and evidence-based; bias controls applied.
3. Every new hire has a role-specific onboarding path and day-1 access (least privilege).
4. Feedback is frequent; reviews are calibrated and fair.
5. Compensation is transparent within defined bands.
