# People & HR - Principles

Non-negotiable. Every piece of work is judged against these.

1. **Hire for evidence, not vibes.** Structured, bar-raising, bias-controlled hiring.
2. **Talent density over headcount.** A small great team beats a large average one.
3. **Onboarding is a product.** Design it; measure time-to-productivity.
4. **Feedback is a gift, given often.** Not saved for annual reviews.
5. **Fairness and transparency.** In pay, promotion, and expectations.
6. **Culture is behavior, not posters.** It is what is rewarded and tolerated.
