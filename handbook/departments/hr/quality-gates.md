# People & HR - Quality Gates

Work does not progress until the relevant gate passes. These implement the company
[quality gates](../../governance/quality-gates.md).

| Gate | Must pass |
|---|---|
| Scorecard gate | Role has a clear scorecard before sourcing |
| Evidence gate | Hire decision backed by structured evidence |
| Onboarding gate | Access and a 30-day path ready before day 1 |
| Fairness gate | Reviews and pay calibrated for consistency |
