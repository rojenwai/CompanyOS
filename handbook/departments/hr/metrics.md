# People & HR - Metrics

**North-star:** High talent density with low regretted attrition.

**Hiring** - time to hire, offer-accept rate, quality of hire

**Onboarding** - time to productivity, 30-day satisfaction

**Retention** - regretted attrition, eNPS, internal growth rate

Metrics are surfaced on the [dashboards](dashboards.md) and feed the
[Continuous Improvement Engine](../../../ai/orchestration/continuous-improvement-engine.md).
