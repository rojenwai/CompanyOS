# People & HR - Roles

| Role | Accountable for |
|---|---|
| Head of People (COO domain) | People strategy, culture, final hiring calibration |
| Recruiter | Sourcing, screening, coordinating hiring |
| People Ops Specialist | Onboarding, HR operations, compensation ops |
| People Development Partner | Performance, growth ladders, culture programs |

Human roles are amplified by the [department agents](agents.md). Every role follows the
[principles](principles.md) and the [review process](review-process.md).
