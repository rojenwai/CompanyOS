# People & HR - Review Process

Hiring decisions are reviewed by the hiring manager and Head of People against the scorecard and the bar. Performance calibration is reviewed for fairness and consistency. Compensation changes are reviewed with finance.

Reviews are independent - the [Reviewer](../../../ai/orchestration/reviewer.md) never assumes the author is
correct. Security-sensitive work also requires the
[Security Reviewer](../../../ai/orchestration/security-reviewer.md).
