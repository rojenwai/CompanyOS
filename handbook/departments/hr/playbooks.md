# People & HR - Playbooks

Situational guides. Each names the trigger and the steps.

### A key person is a retention risk

1. Understand the driver (growth, pay, manager, purpose)
2. Act on what is real and fair
3. Offer a concrete growth or role change
4. Follow through
5. Learn for the broader team

### A role has a weak pipeline

1. Re-check the scorecard and market
2. Broaden sourcing channels
3. Adjust compensation if the market demands
4. Consider an interim agent-augmented solution
5. Track pipeline health
