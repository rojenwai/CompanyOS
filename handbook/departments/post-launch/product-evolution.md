# Product Evolution

Periodically, the company steps back from day-to-day maintenance and asks whether the product is still
the right product.

## Evolution questions

- Is the product still solving the problem?
- Has the market changed?
- Are competitors ahead?
- Should pricing change?
- Should features be removed?
- Should the UI be redesigned?
- Should the architecture be modernized?
- Should the AI models be upgraded?
- Should the company pivot?

## Cadence & ownership

Run on a regular strategic cadence by [product](../product/README.md) and the
[CEO Agent](../../../ai/orchestration/ceo-agent.md), informed by [analytics](analytics.md),
[competitive research](../research/README.md), and [customer feedback](customer-feedback.md).

## Outcomes

Continue · improve · redesign · modernize · [deprecate](deprecation-policy.md) · pivot. Decisions are
recorded to [decision memory](../../../ai/memory/decision-memory.md) and flow into the [roadmap](../product/roadmap.md).
Removing features follows the [deprecation policy](deprecation-policy.md); retiring a product follows
[end-of-life](end-of-life.md).
