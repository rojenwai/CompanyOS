# Post-Launch Metrics

## North-star
**Reliable customer value over time** — proxied by SLA attainment × retention.

## Reliability
Uptime / [SLA](sla.md) attainment · error rate · latency (p50/p95/p99) · MTTR · change-failure rate ·
incident count by severity.

## Maintenance health
Dependency freshness · open security findings · [technical-debt](technical-debt/metrics.md) trend ·
backup success rate · doc-drift count.

## Customer
Support resolution time · CSAT/NPS · churn · feature adoption · renewal rate.

## Cost
Infrastructure cost · cost per customer · cost per request.

Every metric drives a decision; surfaced on [dashboards](dashboards.md).
