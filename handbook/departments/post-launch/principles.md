# Principles

1. **Own the whole life.** A product is supported from launch to a dignified [end-of-life](end-of-life.md), never abandoned.
2. **Detect before customers do.** [Monitoring](monitoring.md) and [observability](observability.md) catch problems first.
3. **Blameless response.** [Incidents](incident-response.md) focus on systems and process, not individuals.
4. **Every failure teaches.** Failure without a recorded lesson is unacceptable ([continuous improvement](continuous-improvement.md)).
5. **Maintain continuously.** Debt, dependencies, and drift are addressed steadily, not in crises ([maintenance](maintenance.md)).
6. **Safe change.** Every release is reversible ([rollback](rollback.md)); irreversible actions need [approval](../../../ai/orchestration/approval-engine.md).
7. **Customer-first recovery.** Communicate honestly and restore service before assigning cause.

These specialize the company [core principles](../../company/principles.md), especially
[Long-Term Thinking](../../company/principles.md) and [Continuous Improvement](../../company/principles.md).
