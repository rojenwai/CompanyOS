# Feature Requests

How customer and internal feature requests are captured, evaluated, and answered.

## Intake

Requests arrive from [support](support.md), [customer feedback](customer-feedback.md), sales, and
internal teams. Each is logged with requester, problem, and frequency.

## Evaluation

1. Deduplicate and cluster by underlying job (not surface ask).
2. Weigh by reach and business value against the [prioritization frameworks](../product/decision-frameworks.md).
3. Validate the underlying problem via [discovery](../product/discovery.md) before committing.

## Response

- **Accepted** → added to the [backlog](../product/roadmap.md) with a success metric.
- **Deferred** → recorded and communicated with rationale.
- **Declined** → closed transparently with the reason.

Requests are a signal, not a spec — we solve the underlying job, not always the literal request.
