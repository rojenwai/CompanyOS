# Documentation - Principles

Non-negotiable. Every piece of work is judged against these.

1. **Docs are part of done.** Nothing ships without its documentation ([definition of done](../../governance/definition-of-done.md)).
2. **Accurate or deleted.** A wrong doc is removed or fixed, never left to mislead.
3. **Write for the reader.** Match their context, task, and expertise.
4. **One home per topic.** Avoid duplicate, diverging docs.
5. **Every doc has an owner and a freshness date** ([documentation memory](../../../ai/memory/documentation-memory.md)).
6. **Show, then tell.** Examples before exhaustive reference.
