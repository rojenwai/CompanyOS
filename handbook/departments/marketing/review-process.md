# Marketing - Review Process

Marketing work is reviewed for accuracy (are claims true?), clarity (is the message sharp?), and economics (is acquisition efficient?). The CMO reviews positioning and brand; the CFO reviews significant spend.

Reviews are independent - the [Reviewer](../../../ai/orchestration/reviewer.md) never assumes the author is
correct. Security-sensitive work also requires the
[Security Reviewer](../../../ai/orchestration/security-reviewer.md).
