# Marketing - Workflows

The core repeatable processes this department runs.

### Launch a product/feature

1. Confirm positioning and messaging
2. Prepare content, PR, and social
3. Coordinate with the [launch playbook](../../playbooks/launching-a-product.md)
4. Publish and amplify
5. Measure impact on the north-star
6. Iterate

### Grow a channel

1. Hypothesize the channel/offer
2. Run a bounded experiment
3. Measure CAC and conversion
4. Scale winners within budget
5. Kill losers
6. Report economics to finance
