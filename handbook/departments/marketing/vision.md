# Marketing - Vision

- Horizon 1 - Sharp positioning and messaging; a working content and SEO engine; instrumented funnel.
- Horizon 2 - Multiple efficient channels with known CAC; repeatable launches that move the north-star.
- Horizon 3 - A brand and community that compound - inbound demand and word of mouth do the work.

The end state: the right customers find the company, understand it, and trust it.
