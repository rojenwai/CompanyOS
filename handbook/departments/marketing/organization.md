# Marketing - Organization

## Reporting

The [CMO](../../company/roles.md) owns branding, growth, marketing, customer acquisition, SEO, social media, and product launches.

```
CMO
|-- Content & SEO       - content engine, organic growth
|-- Brand & Comms       - positioning, PR, social
`-- Growth / Performance - paid, funnel, experiments
```

## Interfaces

| Works with | On |
|---|---|
| [Strategy](../strategy/go-to-market.md) | GTM, segments, positioning |
| [Product](../product/README.md) | Value, launches, messaging accuracy |
| [Sales](../sales/README.md) | Qualified demand handoff |
| [Finance](../finance/README.md) | CAC, budget, channel ROI |

## Where this plugs into the kernel

Work is decomposed and routed by the [coordinator](../../../ai/orchestration/coordinator.md), reviewed at the
[quality gates](quality-gates.md), and its lasting knowledge persists to
[memory](../../../ai/memory/README.md). Major decisions require cross-functional review before
[CEO Agent](../../../ai/orchestration/ceo-agent.md) approval.
