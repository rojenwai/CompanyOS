# DevOps - Vision

- Horizon 1 - Fully automated CI/CD with quality, security, and coverage gates; one-command rollback.
- Horizon 2 - All infrastructure as code; ephemeral environments per change; progressive delivery by default.
- Horizon 3 - Self-healing infrastructure that scales, patches, and recovers within guardrails.

The end state: delivery and reliability are automated well enough to be unremarkable.
