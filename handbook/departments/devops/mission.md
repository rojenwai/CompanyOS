# DevOps - Mission

**Make shipping safe, fast, and boring.**

Deploying should be a non-event: automated, tested, observable, and reversible. DevOps removes the friction and the fear from delivery so engineers ship often and sleep well.

Priority order when they conflict:

1. **Safe** - every change is tested and reversible ([rollback](../post-launch/rollback.md)).
2. **Repeatable** - infrastructure is code, not clicks.
3. **Fast** - short lead time from commit to production.
4. **Observable** - every deploy and system is instrumented.
