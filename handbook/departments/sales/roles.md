# Sales - Roles

| Role | Accountable for |
|---|---|
| Revenue Lead / CEO (early) | Sales strategy, key deals, forecast |
| SDR / Prospecting | Qualifying inbound and outbound demand |
| Account Executive | Discovery, solution, negotiation, close |
| Sales Engineer | Technical fit, demos, proofs of concept |

Human roles are amplified by the [department agents](agents.md). Every role follows the
[principles](principles.md) and the [review process](review-process.md).
