# Sales - Metrics

**North-star:** Efficient new revenue from well-fit customers who stay.

**Pipeline** - qualified pipeline, ICP match, stage conversion

**Closing** - win rate, sales cycle length, average deal size

**Quality** - first-year retention of closed deals, forecast accuracy, discount discipline

Metrics are surfaced on the [dashboards](dashboards.md) and feed the
[Continuous Improvement Engine](../../../ai/orchestration/continuous-improvement-engine.md).
