# Sales - Mission

**Help the right customers buy, and start every relationship honestly.**

Sales is not persuasion for its own sake - it is matching a real problem to a real solution and removing the friction to yes. Founders sell the first ~100 customers themselves to learn what resonates before the motion is systematized.

Priority order when they conflict:

1. **Right fit** - sell to customers the product genuinely helps.
2. **Honesty** - never oversell; set expectations the product will meet.
3. **Value-led** - anchor on the customer's problem and outcome.
4. **Efficiency** - a repeatable, forecastable motion.
