# Sales - Quality Gates

Work does not progress until the relevant gate passes. These implement the company
[quality gates](../../governance/quality-gates.md).

| Gate | Must pass |
|---|---|
| Qualification gate | Opportunity fits the ICP with real need and timing |
| Honesty gate | No overselling; expectations documented |
| Terms gate | Pricing within policy or approved; contract legal-reviewed |
| Handoff gate | Complete handoff to customer-success |
