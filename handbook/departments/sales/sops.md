# Sales - Standard Operating Procedures

### SOP-01 - Qualify an opportunity

1. Confirm fit to the ICP
2. Establish need, timing, and decision process
3. Assess value potential
4. Advance or qualify out
5. Log in CRM

### SOP-02 - Close a deal

1. Confirm value and success criteria
2. Agree terms within policy
3. Route contract to [legal](../legal/README.md)
4. Obtain approvals
5. Hand off to [customer-success](../customer-success/README.md)
