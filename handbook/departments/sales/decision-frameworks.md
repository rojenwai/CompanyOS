# Sales - Decision Frameworks

How decisions are made here. All decisions follow the company
[decision framework](../../governance/decision-framework.md); these are the department-specific ones.

- **Advance vs. qualify out** - Advance only genuine fit with real need and timing; qualify out early otherwise.
- **Discount vs. hold** - Hold on value; discount only for strategic reasons within policy.
- **Custom commitment vs. standard** - Avoid one-off product/legal commitments unless strategically justified and approved.

Reversible decisions are made fast and revisited; irreversible ones require review and are recorded in
[decision memory](../../../ai/memory/decision-memory.md).
