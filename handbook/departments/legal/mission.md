# Legal - Mission

**Enable the company to move fast without stepping on legal landmines.**

Legal is not the department of no - it is the department of *how to safely*. It identifies risk early, structures it, and clears the path so the business can act with confidence.

Priority order when they conflict:

1. **Protect the company** - limit liability and protect assets.
2. **Protect customers** - honest terms and real privacy.
3. **Enable the business** - find the compliant path to the goal.
4. **Preserve optionality** - avoid commitments that trap the company.
