# Legal - Standard Operating Procedures

### SOP-01 - Approve terms for signature

1. Confirm business terms
2. Review against the playbook
3. Flag residual risk
4. Obtain human approval
5. Execute and file

### SOP-02 - Onboard a vendor

1. Review the vendor agreement and DPA
2. Check data and security terms with [security](../security/README.md)
3. Negotiate risky clauses
4. Approve and record obligations
5. Track renewal
