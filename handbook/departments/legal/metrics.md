# Legal - Metrics

**North-star:** Zero avoidable legal incidents, with fast, safe enablement of the business.

**Enablement** - contract turnaround, deals unblocked, time-to-advice

**Protection** - IP protected vs. at risk, license violations caught, risk issues flagged pre-signature

**Compliance** - obligation coverage, regulatory findings, gap-closure time

Metrics are surfaced on the [dashboards](dashboards.md) and feed the
[Continuous Improvement Engine](../../../ai/orchestration/continuous-improvement-engine.md).
