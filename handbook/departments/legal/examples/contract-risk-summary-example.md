# Example - Contract Risk Summary

> Worked example - illustrative, not a live record.

**Agreement:** Enterprise customer MSA.

| Term | Assessment | Recommendation |
|---|---|---|
| Liability cap | Uncapped for data breach | Negotiate cap at 12 months fees + carve-out |
| IP | Customer claims ownership of feedback | Revise to license-back of feedback |
| Data | No DPA attached | Attach standard DPA before signature |
| Term | Auto-renew, 90-day notice | Acceptable |

**Outcome:** Two blocking items (uncapped liability, missing DPA) must resolve before signature; routed to the CLO for approval once redlined.
