# Operations - Mission

**Make the whole company run smoothly, predictably, and without friction.**

Great teams stall on bad coordination. Operations designs the processes, cadences, and systems that let work flow - so decisions get made, projects ship, and nothing falls through the cracks.

Priority order when they conflict:

1. **Alignment** - everyone knows the goal, the plan, and their part.
2. **Flow** - remove blockers; keep work moving.
3. **Predictability** - reliable cadences and commitments.
4. **Leverage** - the right process and tools, no more.
