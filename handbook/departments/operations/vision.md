# Operations - Vision

- Horizon 1 - Clear cadences (daily to quarterly) and a single source of truth for status.
- Horizon 2 - Lightweight, automated processes; blockers surfaced and resolved fast.
- Horizon 3 - A self-documenting operation where status, decisions, and knowledge are always current.

The end state: the company runs like a well-tuned machine, and no one feels the gears.
