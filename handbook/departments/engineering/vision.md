# Vision

An engineering organization where quality compounds: every system is reliable, every change is safe,
and every engineer (human or AI) can confidently work in code they did not write.

## 3-horizon view

| Horizon | Timeframe | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| H1 | 0–2 years | Production-quality delivery on the current stack; full test and review discipline |
| H2 | 2–5 years | Platform maturity — reusable services, strong automation, low change-failure rate |
| H3 | 5–10+ years | Self-maintaining systems where [post-launch agents](../post-launch/README.md) keep quality high without active development |

Excellence is defined in the [department README](README.md#definition-of-excellence).
