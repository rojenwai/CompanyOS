# Customer Success - Vision

- Horizon 1 - Every customer onboarded to first value fast, with a health score and an owner.
- Horizon 2 - Proactive, data-driven success - churn predicted and prevented before it happens.
- Horizon 3 - Expansion and advocacy as the primary growth engine, powered by proven outcomes.

The end state: net revenue retention above 100% - customers grow with the company.
