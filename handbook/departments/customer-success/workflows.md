# Customer Success - Workflows

The core repeatable processes this department runs.

### Onboard to renewal (customer lifecycle)

1. Receive the [sales](../sales/README.md) handoff
2. Onboard to first value
3. Drive adoption and monitor health
4. Intervene on risk proactively
5. Renew on delivered value
6. Expand and capture advocacy

### Prevent a churn

1. Detect the risk via health score
2. Diagnose the root cause
3. Intervene with a concrete plan
4. Deliver the missing value
5. Confirm recovery and update the model
