# Example - Account Health Score

> Worked example - illustrative, not a live record.

**Account:** Mid-size SaaS (renewal in 75 days).

| Signal | Weight | Value | Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| Weekly active usage | 40% | 62/100 | Down from 85 last month |
| Feature breadth | 20% | 70/100 | Core only; not using integrations |
| Support sentiment | 20% | 55/100 | Two frustrated tickets |
| Exec engagement | 20% | 40/100 | Champion left the company |

**Score: 57/100 (Yellow -> trending Red).** Trigger: champion loss + usage drop. **Action:** exec re-engagement + integration enablement within 2 weeks, ahead of renewal.
