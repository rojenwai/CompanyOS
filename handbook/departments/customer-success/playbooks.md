# Customer Success - Playbooks

Situational guides. Each names the trigger and the steps.

### An account goes red

1. Confirm the signal (usage, support, sentiment)
2. Reach out and diagnose
3. Agree a recovery plan with owners and dates
4. Deliver and follow up
5. Escalate to leadership if unrecoverable

### A customer requests a missing feature

1. Understand the underlying outcome
2. Offer a current workaround
3. Log to [feature requests](../post-launch/feature-requests.md)
4. Set honest expectations
5. Follow up when shipped
