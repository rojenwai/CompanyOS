# Customer Success - Roles

| Role | Accountable for |
|---|---|
| Head of Customer Success | Retention strategy, health model, escalations |
| Onboarding Specialist | Time-to-first-value for new customers |
| Customer Success Manager | Account health, adoption, retention |
| Renewals / Expansion Manager | Renewals, upsell, advocacy |

Human roles are amplified by the [department agents](agents.md). Every role follows the
[principles](principles.md) and the [review process](review-process.md).
