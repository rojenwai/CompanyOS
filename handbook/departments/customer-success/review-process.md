# Customer Success - Review Process

Success work is reviewed for outcomes (are customers achieving value?), health accuracy (does the score predict reality?), and retention (are renewals value-based?). The Head of CS reviews at-risk accounts; finance reviews retention and expansion.

Reviews are independent - the [Reviewer](../../../ai/orchestration/reviewer.md) never assumes the author is
correct. Security-sensitive work also requires the
[Security Reviewer](../../../ai/orchestration/security-reviewer.md).
