# Customer Success - Organization

## Reporting

The [COO](../../company/roles.md); revenue retention is shared with finance/revenue leadership.

```
COO
|-- Onboarding      - time-to-first-value
|-- Success Mgmt    - health, adoption, retention
`-- Renewals & Expansion - renewal, upsell, advocacy
```

## Interfaces

| Works with | On |
|---|---|
| [Sales](../sales/README.md) | Handoffs, expansion, references |
| [Product](../product/README.md) | Adoption, feedback, feature requests |
| [Post-launch](../post-launch/support.md) | Support tickets, incidents affecting customers |
| [Finance](../finance/README.md) | Renewals, expansion revenue, churn |

## Where this plugs into the kernel

Work is decomposed and routed by the [coordinator](../../../ai/orchestration/coordinator.md), reviewed at the
[quality gates](quality-gates.md), and its lasting knowledge persists to
[memory](../../../ai/memory/README.md). Major decisions require cross-functional review before
[CEO Agent](../../../ai/orchestration/ceo-agent.md) approval.
