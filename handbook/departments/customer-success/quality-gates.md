# Customer Success - Quality Gates

Work does not progress until the relevant gate passes. These implement the company
[quality gates](../../governance/quality-gates.md).

| Gate | Must pass |
|---|---|
| Onboarding gate | Customer reached first value; success criteria confirmed |
| Health gate | Every account scored on real usage |
| Renewal gate | Value quantified before the renewal conversation |
| Feedback gate | Signal routed to product |
