# Customer Success - Agents

The Customer Success agents, each written from the [agent template](../../../ai/agents/agent-template.md) and living
in [agents/customer-success/](../../../ai/agents/customer-success/).

| Agent | Mission (one line) |
|---|---|
| [Customer Onboarding Agent](../../../ai/agents/customer-success/customer-onboarding-agent.md) | Get every new customer to first value as fast as possible. |
| [Support Agent](../../../ai/agents/customer-success/support-agent.md) | Resolve customer issues quickly and accurately, and turn them into product signal. |
| [Customer Health Agent](../../../ai/agents/customer-success/customer-health-agent.md) | Score account health and predict churn early enough to prevent it. |
| [Renewal & Expansion Agent](../../../ai/agents/customer-success/renewal-expansion-agent.md) | Secure renewals on delivered value and grow accounts where more value exists. |

All agent output is reviewed per [review-process.md](review-process.md); major decisions escalate to the
[CEO Agent](../../../ai/orchestration/ceo-agent.md).
