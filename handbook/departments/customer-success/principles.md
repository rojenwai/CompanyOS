# Customer Success - Principles

Non-negotiable. Every piece of work is judged against these.

1. **Outcomes over activity.** Success is the customer's result, not our touchpoints.
2. **Proactive, not reactive.** Reach out before the customer struggles.
3. **Health is measured.** Every account has a health score based on real usage.
4. **Honesty in renewals.** Renew on delivered value, not lock-in.
5. **Feedback is fuel.** Every interaction feeds [product](../post-launch/customer-feedback.md).
6. **Fit customers first.** Focus effort where the product genuinely helps.
