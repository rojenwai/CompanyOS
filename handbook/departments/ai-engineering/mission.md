# AI Engineering - Mission

**Build AI systems that are reliable, evaluable, and safe - not demos.**

The objective is intelligent systems that solve real problems, reason correctly, use tools effectively, minimize hallucination, and improve continuously - never chatbots that impress once and fail in production.

Priority order when they conflict:

1. **Reliable** - correct and consistent under real inputs.
2. **Evaluable** - quality is measured before it ships ([evaluation-and-testing.md](evaluation-and-testing.md)).
3. **Safe** - hardened against misuse and leakage ([ai-security.md](ai-security.md)).
4. **Cost-efficient** - the smallest sufficient model, grounded and cached.
