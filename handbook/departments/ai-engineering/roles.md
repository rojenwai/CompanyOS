# AI Engineering - Roles

| Role | Accountable for |
|---|---|
| AI Architect | Model selection, serving, and training strategy |
| LLM / Applied AI Engineer | Prompts, RAG, agents, tool calling, memory |
| ML Engineer | Training, inference, and deployment of task models |
| AI Evaluation Lead | Evaluation datasets, metrics, and release gating |

Human roles are amplified by the [department agents](agents.md). Every role follows the
[principles](principles.md) and the [review process](review-process.md).
