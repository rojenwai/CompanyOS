# Research - Agents

The Research agents, each written from the [agent template](../../../ai/agents/agent-template.md) and living
in [agents/research/](../../../ai/agents/research/).

| Agent | Mission (one line) |
|---|---|
| [Problem Discovery Agent](../../../ai/agents/research/problem-discovery-agent.md) | Surface and validate significant, underserved problems worth building a company around. |
| [Market Research Agent](../../../ai/agents/research/market-research-agent.md) | Size the market and quantify demand for a validated problem. |
| [Competitor Analysis Agent](../../../ai/agents/research/competitor-analysis-agent.md) | Map the competitive landscape and find defensible positioning gaps. |
| [Opportunity Ranking Agent](../../../ai/agents/research/opportunity-ranking-agent.md) | Score and rank opportunities so the company pursues the highest-value bets first. |

All agent output is reviewed per [review-process.md](review-process.md); major decisions escalate to the
[CEO Agent](../../../ai/orchestration/ceo-agent.md).
