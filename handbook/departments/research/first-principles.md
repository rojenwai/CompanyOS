# First Principles Framework

Instead of asking *"How do competitors solve this?"* ask *"What are the fundamental constraints?"*

## Method

Break every problem down into its fundamentals:

- **Physics** — what the laws of nature allow.
- **Mathematics** — what is provably possible/optimal.
- **Economics** — what the cost structure really is.
- **Human behavior** — what people actually do and want.

Then **rebuild from first principles**, rather than copying existing solutions.

This is the research expression of the company principle
[First Principles Thinking](../../company/principles.md) and drives
[opportunity discovery](opportunity-scoring.md).
