# Research - Principles

Non-negotiable. Every piece of work is judged against these.

1. **Evidence over opinion.** Every claim carries a source, a confidence, and a date.
2. **Distinguish fact, assumption, and prediction.** Never blur them.
3. **First principles.** Break the problem to fundamentals before proposing solutions ([first-principles.md](first-principles.md)).
4. **Significance first.** A rigorous answer to an unimportant question is wasted effort.
5. **Respect IP.** Understand principles; never copy protected work ([research-ethics.md](research-ethics.md)).
6. **Reproducibility.** Another agent should reach the same conclusion from your sources.
