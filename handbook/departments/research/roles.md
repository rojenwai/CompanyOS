# Research - Roles

| Role | Accountable for |
|---|---|
| Chief Scientist | Research direction, scientific validation, final research review |
| Problem Discovery Researcher | Finding and validating significant problems |
| Market Researcher | Demand, sizing, segments, willingness to pay |
| Competitive Analyst | Competitor and patent landscape, positioning gaps |
| Technology Scout | Emerging technologies and their applicability |

Human roles are amplified by the [department agents](agents.md). Every role follows the
[principles](principles.md) and the [review process](review-process.md).
