# Research - Mission

**Find the problems worth solving before anyone else does.**

Research exists to reduce the company's biggest risk - building the wrong thing. It converts curiosity into evidence and evidence into ranked, buildable opportunities.

Priority order when they conflict:

1. **Truth** - never fabricate or overstate evidence ([research-ethics.md](research-ethics.md)).
2. **Significance** - pursue problems that matter to many people.
3. **Feasibility** - separate what is possible from what is merely interesting.
4. **Defensibility** - favor opportunities that build durable advantage.
