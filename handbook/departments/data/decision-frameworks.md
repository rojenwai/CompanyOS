# Data - Decision Frameworks

How decisions are made here. All decisions follow the company
[decision framework](../../governance/decision-framework.md); these are the department-specific ones.

- **Real-time vs. batch** - Use real-time only when the decision needs it; batch otherwise for simplicity and cost.
- **Centralize vs. self-serve** - Govern definitions centrally; enable self-serve consumption on the governed layer.
- **Collect vs. minimize** - Collect only data with a clear use; minimize and govern sensitive data.

Reversible decisions are made fast and revisited; irreversible ones require review and are recorded in
[decision memory](../../../ai/memory/decision-memory.md).
