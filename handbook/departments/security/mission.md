# Security - Mission

**Make security a property of how we build, not a gate we bolt on.**

One breach can end a company's trust. Security's job is to reduce risk continuously - designing systems that are hard to compromise, finding weaknesses before attackers do, and responding fast when something slips through.

Priority order when they conflict:

1. **Protect customer data** - confidentiality, integrity, availability.
2. **Prevent** - secure design and defaults beat detection.
3. **Detect** - assume breach; instrument to notice fast.
4. **Respond** - contain, eradicate, recover, and learn.
