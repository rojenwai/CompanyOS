# Strategy - Workflows

The core repeatable processes this department runs.

### Opportunity to business plan

1. Take a ranked opportunity from [research](../research/README.md)
2. Size the market and segments
3. Choose the business model and pricing
4. Model unit economics
5. Define GTM
6. Approve at the CEO/CFO level

### Path to scale

1. Confirm PMF via [product metrics](../product/metrics.md)
2. Validate repeatable acquisition
3. Confirm healthy unit economics
4. Plan the scaling investment
5. Execute and monitor
