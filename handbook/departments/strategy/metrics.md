# Strategy - Metrics

**North-star:** Long-term enterprise value - a growing, defensible, profitable business.

**Growth** - revenue growth, new customers, expansion revenue

**Economics** - LTV/CAC, gross margin, payback period, runway

**Position** - market share, win rate, PMF indicators

Metrics are surfaced on the [dashboards](dashboards.md) and feed the
[Continuous Improvement Engine](../../../ai/orchestration/continuous-improvement-engine.md).
