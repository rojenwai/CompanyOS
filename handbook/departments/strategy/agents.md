# Strategy - Agents

The Strategy agents, each written from the [agent template](../../../ai/agents/agent-template.md) and living
in [agents/strategy/](../../../ai/agents/strategy/).

| Agent | Mission (one line) |
|---|---|
| [Business Strategy Agent](../../../ai/agents/strategy/business-strategy-agent.md) | Recommend the company's strategic moves - what to pursue, when, and why it is defensible. |
| [Business Model & Pricing Agent](../../../ai/agents/strategy/business-model-pricing-agent.md) | Design how the company creates and captures value, and price it defensibly. |
| [Go-To-Market Agent](../../../ai/agents/strategy/go-to-market-agent.md) | Define how the product reaches and wins its market. |
| [Fundraising Agent](../../../ai/agents/strategy/fundraising-agent.md) | Prepare the company to raise capital on the best terms - or chart a path without it. |

All agent output is reviewed per [review-process.md](review-process.md); major decisions escalate to the
[CEO Agent](../../../ai/orchestration/ceo-agent.md).
