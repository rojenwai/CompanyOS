# Strategy - Tools

Tooling is a means, not an end. Categories used here:

| Category | Examples |
|---|---|
| Modeling | Financial and cap-table modeling |
| Market | Market data, competitive intelligence |
| GTM | Channel analytics, campaign and funnel tooling |
| Planning | Roadmap and OKR tooling |

Tool choices follow the [build vs. buy framework](../engineering/decision-frameworks.md).
