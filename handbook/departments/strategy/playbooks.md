# Strategy - Playbooks

Situational guides. Each names the trigger and the steps.

### No product-market fit yet

1. Re-examine the problem and segment
2. Talk to churned and retained users
3. Sharpen positioning or narrow the segment
4. Change the product or the market - not neither
5. Re-measure PMF before scaling

### Runway getting short

1. Recompute runway to the week
2. Model cut and raise scenarios
3. Prioritize revenue and retention
4. Decide raise, cut, or both
5. Communicate with [finance](../finance/README.md) and the board
