# Strategy - Standards

Testable standards, not aspirations. These extend the company-wide
[standards](../../standards/README.md).

1. Every market claim is sized and sourced.
2. Unit economics are modeled before scaling any channel.
3. Pricing is tied to value delivered, not cost alone.
4. PMF is demonstrated before scaling investment.
5. Strategic decisions are recorded in [decision memory](../../../ai/memory/decision-memory.md).
