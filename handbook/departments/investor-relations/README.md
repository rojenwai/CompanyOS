# Investor Relations

Investor Relations keeps investors and the board accurately informed and engaged, manages reporting and governance, and supports fundraising. Trust with investors is built through consistent, honest communication - good news and bad. It reports to the [CEO](../../company/roles.md) and [CFO](../../company/roles.md).

## Interfaces

Investor Relations draws numbers from [finance](../finance/README.md), the narrative from [strategy](../strategy/README.md), and fundraising support from the [Fundraising Agent](../../../ai/agents/strategy/fundraising-agent.md); it produces board and investor updates and manages governance and the cap table.
