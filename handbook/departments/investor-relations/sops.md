# Investor Relations - Standard Operating Procedures

### SOP-01 - Produce an investor update

1. Gather reconciled metrics and milestones
2. Draft with honest highlights and lowlights
3. Reconcile to finance
4. Obtain sign-off
5. Distribute and archive

### SOP-02 - Record board minutes

1. Capture decisions and rationale
2. Record resolutions and votes
3. List action items and owners
4. Circulate for approval
5. File in the governance record
