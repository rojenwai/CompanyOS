# Investor Relations - Review Process

Investor communications are reviewed for accuracy (do numbers reconcile?), honesty (are lowlights included?), and compliance (is material information handled correctly?). The CEO/CFO approve every external investor communication before it goes out.

Reviews are independent - the [Reviewer](../../../ai/orchestration/reviewer.md) never assumes the author is
correct. Security-sensitive work also requires the
[Security Reviewer](../../../ai/orchestration/security-reviewer.md).
