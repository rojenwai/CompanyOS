# Investor Relations - Playbooks

Situational guides. Each names the trigger and the steps.

### A bad quarter

1. Report it early and honestly
2. Explain the cause and the plan
3. Show the data and the ask
4. Engage investors as partners
5. Follow through on the plan

### Preparing to raise

1. Update the data room and metrics
2. Align the narrative with [strategy](../strategy/README.md)
3. Model dilution scenarios
4. Support the [Fundraising Agent](../../../ai/agents/strategy/fundraising-agent.md)
5. Keep investors warm with consistent updates
