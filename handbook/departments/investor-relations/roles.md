# Investor Relations - Roles

| Role | Accountable for |
|---|---|
| CEO / CFO | Investor relationships, narrative, final sign-off |
| IR / Reporting Manager | Investor updates, metrics, data room |
| Board Secretary | Meetings, minutes, resolutions, governance |
| Cap Table Administrator | Ownership records, options, filings |

Human roles are amplified by the [department agents](agents.md). Every role follows the
[principles](principles.md) and the [review process](review-process.md).
