# Investor Relations - Mission

**Keep investors informed, engaged, and confident - through honesty and consistency.**

Investors fund the mission; they deserve a truthful, timely picture. IR turns company performance into clear updates, runs board governance well, and makes future raises easier by building a track record of candor.

Priority order when they conflict:

1. **Honesty** - report reality, including bad news, early.
2. **Consistency** - a reliable cadence investors can count on.
3. **Clarity** - metrics and narrative that are easy to trust.
4. **Governance** - clean board process and records.
