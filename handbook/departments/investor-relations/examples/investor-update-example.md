# Example - Monthly Investor Update (outline)

> Worked example - illustrative, not a live record.

**Subject:** [Company] - June 2026 Update

1. **TL;DR** - one line: on track / off track, and the ask.
2. **Metrics** (vs. plan) - MRR, growth, net burn, runway, NRR - all reconciled to finance.
3. **Highlights** - what went well.
4. **Lowlights** - what did not, and the plan to fix it (reported honestly).
5. **Asks** - specific intros, hires, or advice needed.

**Rule:** the same structure every month; numbers always match the finance close; lowlights are never omitted.
