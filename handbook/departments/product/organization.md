# Organization

Product reports to the [CPO](../../company/roles.md) and is executed by the [product agents](agents.md).

## Roles in the product organization

| Role | Owns |
|---|---|
| **Chief Product Officer** | Product vision, strategy, roadmaps, prioritization, product-market fit, portfolio |
| **Product Manager** | Requirements, roadmap, customer needs, business goals, KPIs, feature prioritization, stakeholder communication |
| **Technical Product Manager** | Engineering coordination, architecture alignment, scalability, technical debt, platform strategy |
| **Product Operations** | Documentation, metrics, processes, release planning, cross-functional coordination |

## Interfaces

| Interfaces with | For | Via |
|---|---|---|
| [engineering/](../engineering/) | build | PRDs, user stories, [Definition of Ready](../engineering/definition-of-ready.md) |
| [design/](../design/) | UX/UI | discovery, prototypes, design system |
| [research/](../research/) | evidence | market and customer research |
| [post-launch/](../post-launch/) | feedback loop | analytics, feature requests, iteration |
