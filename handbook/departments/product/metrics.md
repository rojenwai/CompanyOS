# Product Metrics & Product-Market Fit

## North-star metric

The single metric that best captures the value delivered to customers. Every other metric supports it.

## Core metrics

North Star · activation rate · retention rate · feature adoption · DAU · MAU · churn · conversion
rate · revenue · customer satisfaction (CSAT/NPS).

## Product-market fit indicators

- High retention
- Organic referrals
- Strong engagement
- Low churn
- Customers would be **very disappointed** if the product disappeared
- Willingness to pay

## Rules

Every metric must drive a decision. Watch for vanity metrics (raw signups, pageviews) that move
without customer value moving. PMF is the gate before [scaling](../strategy/README.md).
