# Vision & Strategy

## Product vision

Every product must define: mission · target customer · core problem · value proposition ·
competitive advantage · long-term vision · success metrics.

## Product strategy

Every strategy answers:

- Who are we building for?
- Why will customers choose us?
- How do we win?
- What differentiates us?
- Where will we be in five years?

## 3-horizon view

| Horizon | Timeframe | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| H1 | 0–2 years | Reach and deepen [product-market fit](metrics.md) |
| H2 | 2–5 years | Expand to adjacent segments and jobs |
| H3 | 5–10+ years | Category leadership and durable moats ([strategy](../strategy/README.md)) |
