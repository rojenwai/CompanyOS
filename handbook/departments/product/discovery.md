# Product Discovery

Discovery precedes development. We validate that a problem is real and worth solving before we build.

## Activities

Customer interviews · observations · journey mapping · competitive analysis · usability testing ·
prototype validation · market research.

## Customer research

Understand: goals · motivations · behaviors · pain points · decision process · environment ·
constraints · current workflow.

## Jobs To Be Done (JTBD)

What job is the customer hiring the product to do? Document:

- **Functional job** — the practical task.
- **Emotional job** — how they want to feel.
- **Social job** — how they want to be perceived.
- **Desired outcome** and **current alternatives**.

## Output

A validated problem statement, target [personas](user-personas.md), the [customer journey](customer-journey.md),
and evidence stored in [research memory](../../../ai/memory/research-memory.md) — enough to justify a
[PRD](requirements.md). Undervalidated ideas are archived, not built.
