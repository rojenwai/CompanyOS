# Product Analytics & Experimentation

## Product analytics

Track: activation · retention · engagement · conversion · session duration · feature adoption ·
churn · customer lifetime value · Net Promoter Score.

Instrumentation is defined with the [Data division](../../../ai/memory/README.md) and surfaced on
[dashboards](dashboards.md).

## A/B testing

Every experiment defines: hypothesis · target audience · variants · metrics · duration · success
threshold · decision criteria.

## Rules

- Decide **before** the test what result will change what decision.
- Run to significance; don't peek-and-stop.
- Record outcomes (including negative results) to [research memory](../../../ai/memory/research-memory.md) and
  the [continuous improvement loop](../../../ai/orchestration/continuous-improvement-engine.md).
