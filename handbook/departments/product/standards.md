# Product Standards

## Requirement quality

Every [requirement and story](requirements.md) is specific, measurable, testable, complete, and
unambiguous. Acceptance criteria map to tests. Non-functional requirements are explicit, not assumed.

## PRD quality

A PRD is not done until it states goals **and** non-goals, success metrics, and risks. It cites
[discovery](discovery.md) evidence — no PRD is built on assumption alone.

## Prioritization discipline

Priority calls use a named [framework](decision-frameworks.md) and record the inputs, so decisions
are auditable and revisable.

## Definition of done for product work

A product deliverable is done when it is validated, prioritized, defined to [Ready](../engineering/definition-of-ready.md),
and its success metric is instrumented.
