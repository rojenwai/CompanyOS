# Hardware - Review Process

Hardware is reviewed for safety (fully tested?), reliability (validated against real conditions?), and manufacturability (buildable at quality and cost?). Design review precedes fabrication; safety failures block shipment and cannot be silently overridden.

Reviews are independent - the [Reviewer](../../../ai/orchestration/reviewer.md) never assumes the author is
correct. Security-sensitive work also requires the
[Security Reviewer](../../../ai/orchestration/security-reviewer.md).
