# Hardware - Decision Frameworks

How decisions are made here. All decisions follow the company
[decision framework](../../governance/decision-framework.md); these are the department-specific ones.

- **Firmware fix vs. hardware revision** - Prefer a firmware fix when it fully resolves the issue; revise hardware only when necessary.
- **Build vs. source a component** - Source proven components unless custom design is genuinely required.
- **Ship vs. hold on a defect** - Hold on any safety or reliability failure; ship only when tests pass.

Reversible decisions are made fast and revisited; irreversible ones require review and are recorded in
[decision memory](../../../ai/memory/decision-memory.md).
