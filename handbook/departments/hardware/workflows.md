# Hardware - Workflows

The core repeatable processes this department runs.

### Hardware design to production

1. Specify requirements (incl. safety and environment)
2. Design (electrical, mechanical, firmware)
3. Review the design
4. Prototype and test (bench, environmental, HIL)
5. Design for manufacture
6. Pilot build and validate
7. Release to production

### Ship a firmware update

1. Test on bench and hardware-in-the-loop
2. Stage a canary fleet with rollback
3. Verify field health
4. Roll out progressively
5. Monitor and be ready to recover
