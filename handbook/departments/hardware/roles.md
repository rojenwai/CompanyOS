# Hardware - Roles

| Role | Accountable for |
|---|---|
| Head of Hardware (CTO domain) | Hardware architecture, safety, final design review |
| Firmware / Embedded Engineer | On-device software and updates |
| Electrical Engineer | Circuits, power, sensors, PCB |
| Mechanical Engineer | Enclosure, structure, thermal, DFM |

Human roles are amplified by the [department agents](agents.md). Every role follows the
[principles](principles.md) and the [review process](review-process.md).
