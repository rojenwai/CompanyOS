# Hardware - Standards

Testable standards, not aspirations. These extend the company-wide
[standards](../../standards/README.md).

1. Safety-critical requirements are fully tested before any shipment.
2. Every design is specified and reviewed before fabrication.
3. Firmware is versioned, tested, and updatable with a recovery path (no bricking).
4. Designs are validated against environmental and durability requirements.
5. Designs are for manufacture, assembly, and service.
