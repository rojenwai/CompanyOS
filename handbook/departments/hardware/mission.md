# Hardware - Mission

**Build physical products that are safe, reliable, manufacturable, and maintainable.**

Hardware failures are expensive and sometimes dangerous, and iteration is slow and costly. This department applies the same engineering rigor as software - specs, review, testing, versioning - to systems where mistakes ship in physical units.

Priority order when they conflict:

1. **Safety** - the product is safe for its users and environment.
2. **Reliability** - it works over its full lifetime and conditions.
3. **Manufacturability** - it can be built at quality and cost.
4. **Maintainability** - it can be updated and serviced in the field.
