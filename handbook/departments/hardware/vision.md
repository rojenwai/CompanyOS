# Hardware - Vision

- Horizon 1 - Every design specified, reviewed, and tested before fabrication; firmware updatable in the field.
- Horizon 2 - Hardware-in-the-loop testing and design-for-manufacture baked into the process.
- Horizon 3 - Fleets that self-report health and update safely, maintained largely by agents.

The end state: physical products as dependable and updatable as good software.
