# Hardware - Principles

Non-negotiable. Every piece of work is judged against these.

1. **Safety is non-negotiable.** Design, test, and certify for it.
2. **Specify before you build.** Fabrication is expensive; catch errors in design and review.
3. **Design for manufacture and service.** Buildable, testable, repairable.
4. **Firmware is software** - versioned, tested, and updatable ([engineering standards](../engineering/standards.md)).
5. **Test in the real world.** Bench, environmental, and hardware-in-the-loop.
6. **Field updates are reversible.** Never brick a device; always have a recovery path.
