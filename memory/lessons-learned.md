# Lessons Learned

**Scope:** what worked and what failed, company-wide, long-lived.

## What's stored
For each completed project, incident, or experiment: what worked, what failed, technical debt created, customer feedback, and process improvements.

## Lifetime
Permanent — this is the compounding asset of the company.

## Writers / readers
Written by the [Continuous Improvement Engine](../orchestration/continuous-improvement-engine.md) after every outcome; read by the [Planner](../orchestration/planner.md) so past mistakes are not repeated.

## Rule
**Failure without a recorded lesson is unacceptable.** Every retrospective and [incident report](../templates/documents/incident-report.md) contributes here.
