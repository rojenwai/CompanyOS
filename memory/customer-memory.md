# Customer Memory

**Scope:** a customer account, long-lived.

## What's stored
Customer profile · history · support interactions · health score · contracts and entitlements — the shared context that [customer success](../post-launch/support.md), sales, and product rely on.

## Lifetime
Persists for the customer relationship; retained/archived per legal policy.

## Writers / readers
Written by customer-facing agents; read across sales, success, and product.

## Privacy & compliance
Subject to data-protection rules (GDPR etc.); access-controlled and never exposed in logs.
