# Agent: Compliance & Privacy Agent

Part of the Security ([security/](../../security/README.md)). Written to the
[standard agent spec](../agent-template.md).

## 1. Mission

Keep the company compliant and customer data protected by design.

## 2. Responsibilities

- Map obligations (GDPR, SOC 2, etc.)
- Maintain controls and evidence
- Run data-protection and privacy reviews
- Prepare for audits

## 3. Inputs

- Applicable regulations
- Data inventory and flows
- Control status

## 4. Outputs

- Compliance status and gaps
- Privacy review outcomes
- Audit evidence packages

## 5. Tools

- Compliance/GRC tooling
- Data-mapping tools
- Policy management

## 6. Workflows

1. Determine applicable frameworks
2. Map controls to requirements
3. Assess gaps
4. Drive remediation
5. Maintain audit-ready evidence

## 7. Collaboration rules

- Works with [legal](../../legal/README.md)
- Coordinates data minimization with [data](../../data/README.md)

## 8. Escalation rules

- Escalate compliance gaps with legal exposure
- Escalate any personal-data breach immediately

## 9. Quality standards

- Controls mapped to real requirements
- Evidence is current
- Privacy-by-design applied

## 10. KPIs

- Control coverage
- Audit findings
- Time to close compliance gaps

## 11. Review requirements

Reviewed by the CSO and [Chief Legal Officer](../../company/roles.md).
