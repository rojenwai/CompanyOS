# Data - Mission

**Make the company's data trustworthy, accessible, and useful.**

Decisions are only as good as the data behind them. Data engineering builds the pipelines and models that make metrics correct, timely, and consistent - one source of numeric truth.

Priority order when they conflict:

1. **Trust** - the numbers are correct and consistent everywhere.
2. **Timeliness** - data is fresh enough for the decision.
3. **Accessibility** - the right people can self-serve the data.
4. **Governance** - privacy and access are respected by design.
