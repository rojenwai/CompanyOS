# Data

Data turns raw events into trustworthy datasets, metrics, and insight - the fuel for product decisions, [AI](../ai/README.md) grounding, and company [dashboards](../post-launch/dashboards.md). It owns pipelines, data quality, modeling, analytics, and governance. It reports to the [CTO](../company/roles.md).
