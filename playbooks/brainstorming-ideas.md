# Playbook: Brainstorming Ideas

**When to use:** you want to generate a pool of company or product ideas to pursue. This is Stage 0, before
[idea validation](../strategy/idea-validation.md). See the full method in [research/ideation.md](../research/ideation.md).

1. **Frame the question** - broad but focused: "How might we remove the pain of X for Y?"
2. **Mine sources of pain** - your own frustrations, observed inefficiencies, forums, reviews, and recent enabling shifts (new AI, hardware, regulation) ([ideation.md](../research/ideation.md#where-good-ideas-actually-come-from)).
3. **Diverge** - generate dozens of ideas with no judgment; quantity over quality; wild ideas welcome; use [first principles](../research/first-principles.md), constraint-relaxation, and cross-pollination.
4. **Cluster** - group raw ideas into themes.
5. **Converge** - score the strongest with [opportunity-scoring](../research/opportunity-scoring.md).
6. **Shortlist** - pick the top 3-5, each framed as a *problem*, and hand them to the [Opportunity Ranking Agent](../agents/research/opportunity-ranking-agent.md).

**Success criteria:** a scored shortlist of problem-framed ideas ready to validate.
**Next:** [validating-an-idea.md](validating-an-idea.md). **Avoid:** falling in love with the first idea, or starting solution-first.
