# Playbook: Hiring an Engineer

**When to use:** you need to add engineering capacity and have approved headcount.

1. **Confirm the need and budget** - approve headcount with [finance](../finance/README.md); confirm the role is the right lever (vs. agents or scope cuts).
2. **Write the scorecard** - mission and first-year outcomes ([role scorecard example](../hr/examples/scorecard-example.md)).
3. **Source** - the [Recruiting Agent](../agents/hr/recruiting-agent.md) sources against the scorecard; the hiring manager reviews the shortlist.
4. **Screen** - structured screen for the top competencies; qualify out early.
5. **Interview loop** - structured, competency-mapped interviews; a real work sample over trivia ([engineering review bar](../engineering/review-process.md)).
6. **Debrief on evidence** - written feedback first, then discuss; hire only on a clear bar match ([hiring checklist](../hr/checklists/hiring-checklist.md)).
7. **Close** - reference checks; [offer](../templates/documents/offer-letter.md) approved with finance; employment agreement via [legal](../legal/README.md).
8. **Onboard** - hand to the [Onboarding Agent](../agents/hr/onboarding-agent.md); day-1 access (least privilege) and a 30-day path.

**Success criteria:** a bar-raising hire, productive within 30 days.
**Escalate if:** the pipeline is weak → the [weak-pipeline playbook](../hr/playbooks.md); pressure to hire below bar → no-hire.
