# Customer Success - Decision Frameworks

How decisions are made here. All decisions follow the company
[decision framework](../governance/decision-framework.md); these are the department-specific ones.

- **Invest vs. let churn** - Invest in fit customers with recoverable value; let genuinely poor-fit accounts go gracefully.
- **Expand now vs. later** - Expand only when the customer is healthy and the added value is real.
- **Escalate vs. handle** - Escalate red accounts and systemic issues; handle routine adoption in-team.

Reversible decisions are made fast and revisited; irreversible ones require review and are recorded in
[decision memory](../memory/decision-memory.md).
