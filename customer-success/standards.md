# Customer Success - Standards

Testable standards, not aspirations. These extend the company-wide
[standards](../standards/README.md).

1. Every customer has an owner, success criteria, and a health score.
2. Onboarding drives to first value on a defined timeline.
3. Outreach is proactive - risk is addressed before renewal.
4. Renewals are based on delivered value, not lock-in.
5. Every interaction feeds [customer feedback](../post-launch/customer-feedback.md).
