# Customer Success - Mission

**Make every customer measurably successful, so they stay and grow.**

A sale is the start of the relationship, not the end. Customer Success ensures customers reach the outcome they bought the product for - then deepen their use over time.

Priority order when they conflict:

1. **Customer outcomes** - they achieve the value they expected.
2. **Retention** - keep customers who are a fit; reduce churn.
3. **Expansion** - grow accounts where more value exists.
4. **Advocacy** - turn success into referrals and references.
