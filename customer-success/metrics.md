# Customer Success - Metrics

**North-star:** Net revenue retention above 100%.

**Adoption** - time to first value, activation rate, feature adoption

**Retention** - gross revenue retention, logo churn, churn prediction accuracy

**Growth** - net revenue retention, expansion revenue, advocacy/referrals

Metrics are surfaced on the [dashboards](dashboards.md) and feed the
[Continuous Improvement Engine](../orchestration/continuous-improvement-engine.md).
