# Legal - Principles

Non-negotiable. Every piece of work is judged against these.

1. **Advise, don't fabricate.** Legal analysis is grounded; uncertainty is stated, not invented ([failure-handling](../governance/failure-handling.md)).
2. **Protect IP deliberately.** Ownership, licensing, and assignment are explicit.
3. **Honest terms.** Contracts and policies say what the company actually does.
4. **Compliance by design.** Regulatory needs are built in, not retrofitted.
5. **Reversibility.** Prefer terms that preserve the company's options.
6. **Human sign-off on legal commitments.** Binding documents require approval.
