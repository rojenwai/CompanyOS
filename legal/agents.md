# Legal - Agents

The Legal agents, each written from the [agent template](../agents/agent-template.md) and living
in [agents/legal/](../agents/legal/).

| Agent | Mission (one line) |
|---|---|
| [Contract Agent](../agents/legal/contract-agent.md) | Draft, review, and flag risk in contracts so the business can sign with confidence. |
| [IP & Patent Agent](../agents/legal/ip-patent-agent.md) | Protect the company's intellectual property and keep its use of others' IP clean. |
| [Compliance Agent](../agents/legal/compliance-agent.md) | Map regulatory obligations and keep the company on the right side of them. |
| [Privacy & Terms Agent](../agents/legal/privacy-terms-agent.md) | Keep the company's terms honest and its data practices privacy-respecting. |

All agent output is reviewed per [review-process.md](review-process.md); major decisions escalate to the
[CEO Agent](../orchestration/ceo-agent.md).
