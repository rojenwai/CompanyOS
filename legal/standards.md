# Legal - Standards

Testable standards, not aspirations. These extend the company-wide
[standards](../standards/README.md).

1. No binding document is signed without human approval.
2. Legal analysis is grounded; uncertainty is stated, never invented.
3. All company IP is assigned and its licensing is explicit.
4. Published terms and privacy policy match actual practice.
5. Regulatory obligations are tracked in a living register.
