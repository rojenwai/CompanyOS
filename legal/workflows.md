# Legal - Workflows

The core repeatable processes this department runs.

### Review a contract

1. Receive the request and business terms
2. Draft or review against the playbook
3. Flag risks and recommend positions
4. Negotiate redlines
5. Route for human signature
6. Record obligations

### Protect a new invention

1. Identify protectable IP
2. Confirm assignment
3. Assess patent/trademark strategy
4. File or document trade-secret protection
5. Track in the IP register
