# Legal - Playbooks

Situational guides. Each names the trigger and the steps.

### Data breach notification

1. Coordinate with the [security-breach playbook](../post-launch/playbooks/security-breach.md)
2. Determine notification obligations by jurisdiction
3. Draft required notices
4. Meet deadlines
5. Record the response

### Open-source license conflict

1. Identify the conflicting license (e.g. copyleft)
2. Assess exposure
3. Choose remove, replace, or comply
4. Verify resolution before shipping
5. Update the license policy
