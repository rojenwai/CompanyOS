# Legal - Roles

| Role | Accountable for |
|---|---|
| Chief Legal Officer | Legal strategy, risk decisions, final legal review |
| Contracts Counsel | Drafting, review, and negotiation of agreements |
| IP Counsel | Patents, trademarks, licensing, open-source compliance |
| Compliance & Privacy Counsel | Regulatory compliance, privacy, terms of service |

Human roles are amplified by the [department agents](agents.md). Every role follows the
[principles](principles.md) and the [review process](review-process.md).
