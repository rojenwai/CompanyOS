# Legal - Review Process

Legal work is reviewed for soundness (is the analysis grounded in real law and terms?), risk (are exposures flagged and bounded?), and authority (was human approval obtained for binding acts?). The CLO reviews non-standard risk; binding documents are human-signed.

Reviews are independent - the [Reviewer](../orchestration/reviewer.md) never assumes the author is
correct. Security-sensitive work also requires the
[Security Reviewer](../orchestration/security-reviewer.md).
