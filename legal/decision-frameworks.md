# Legal - Decision Frameworks

How decisions are made here. All decisions follow the company
[decision framework](../governance/decision-framework.md); these are the department-specific ones.

- **Standard vs. escalate** - Standard-playbook terms proceed; non-standard risk escalates to the CLO.
- **Patent vs. trade secret** - Patent when disclosure is worth protection; trade-secret when secrecy is more valuable.
- **Accept vs. negotiate risk** - Accept only quantified, bounded risk; negotiate open-ended liability.

Reversible decisions are made fast and revisited; irreversible ones require review and are recorded in
[decision memory](../memory/decision-memory.md).
