## Approvals & Human-in-the-Loop

High-risk and irreversible actions require human approval — see [decision-framework.md](decision-framework.md)
and the [orchestration approval engine](../orchestration/approval-engine.md). Financial commitments,
legal documents, production deployments, security-sensitive actions, and customer-impacting changes
always require a human in the loop.
