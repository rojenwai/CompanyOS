# Strategy - Roles

| Role | Accountable for |
|---|---|
| CEO | Company direction, strategic bets, final approval |
| Business Strategist | Lifecycle stage, positioning, competitive strategy |
| Financial Strategist | Business model, pricing, unit economics, fundraising |
| GTM Lead | Channels, funnel, launch, expansion |

Human roles are amplified by the [department agents](agents.md). Every role follows the
[principles](principles.md) and the [review process](review-process.md).
