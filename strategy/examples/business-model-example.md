# Example - Business Model Decision

> Worked example - illustrative, not a live record.

**Product:** Automated incident postmortems (SaaS).

**Model:** Per-seat SaaS subscription with a usage add-on for incident volume.  
**Pricing:** $40/seat/mo (team tier), $12 per incident over 50/mo.  

| Metric | Value | Note |
|---|---|---|
| ACV (avg) | $9,600 | 20-seat teams |
| CAC | $2,800 | content + founder sales |
| LTV | $34,000 | 30-mo retention, 110% NRR |
| LTV/CAC | 12x | healthy |
| Payback | 6 mo | acceptable |

**Decision:** Model is viable; positive economics support scaling the content channel after PMF is confirmed.
