# Strategy - Decision Frameworks

How decisions are made here. All decisions follow the company
[decision framework](../governance/decision-framework.md); these are the department-specific ones.

- **Scale vs. iterate** - Scale only after PMF, repeatable acquisition, and healthy unit economics.
- **Build vs. buy vs. partner** - For non-core capabilities, prefer buy or partner ([build vs. buy](../engineering/decision-frameworks.md)).
- **Raise vs. bootstrap** - Raise when capital accelerates a defensible, working model - not to fund an unproven one.

Reversible decisions are made fast and revisited; irreversible ones require review and are recorded in
[decision memory](../memory/decision-memory.md).
