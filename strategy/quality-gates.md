# Strategy - Quality Gates

Work does not progress until the relevant gate passes. These implement the company
[quality gates](../governance/quality-gates.md).

| Gate | Must pass |
|---|---|
| Evidence gate | Market sizing and economics are sourced and modeled |
| PMF gate | PMF demonstrated before scaling |
| Economics gate | Positive unit economics or a credible path |
| Approval gate | Irreversible bets approved via the decision framework |
