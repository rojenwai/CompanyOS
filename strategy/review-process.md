# Strategy - Review Process

Strategy is reviewed for evidence (are market and economic claims sourced?), defensibility (does this build a moat?), and mission fit. Fundraising and pricing changes get CFO/CEO review; irreversible bets require the full [decision framework](../governance/decision-framework.md).

Reviews are independent - the [Reviewer](../orchestration/reviewer.md) never assumes the author is
correct. Security-sensitive work also requires the
[Security Reviewer](../orchestration/security-reviewer.md).
