# Strategy - Principles

Non-negotiable. Every piece of work is judged against these.

1. **Problem first, technology second.** Technology is valuable only when it solves a real problem better.
2. **Evidence over narrative.** Market claims are sized and sourced, not asserted.
3. **Unit economics are non-negotiable.** Growth that loses money per customer is not growth.
4. **Defensibility compounds.** Prefer moves that build moats over one-off wins.
5. **PMF gates scaling.** Do not scale before product-market fit ([product metrics](../product/metrics.md)).
6. **Long-term value.** Optimize for the enduring company, not the quarter.
