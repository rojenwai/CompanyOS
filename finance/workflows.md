# Finance - Workflows

The core repeatable processes this department runs.

### Monthly financial cycle

1. Close the books ([accounting](../finance/README.md))
2. Reconcile and produce statements
3. Update the forecast and runway
4. Explain budget variance
5. Report to leadership and the board

### Budget a plan or hire

1. Define the ask and expected return
2. Model the cost against the forecast
3. Check runway impact
4. Get CFO approval
5. Track actuals vs. budget
