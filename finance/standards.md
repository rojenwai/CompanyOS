# Finance - Standards

Testable standards, not aspirations. These extend the company-wide
[standards](../standards/README.md).

1. Runway and burn are current to the week and visible to leadership.
2. Every number is traceable to a source and auditable.
3. Forecasts state their assumptions; projections are never presented as facts.
4. Financial commitments require the appropriate human approval.
5. The books are closed on a fixed monthly schedule.
