# Finance - Roles

| Role | Accountable for |
|---|---|
| CFO | Financial strategy, runway, fundraising, final financial approval |
| FP&A Analyst | Forecasting, budgeting, variance, unit economics |
| Accountant | Bookkeeping, close, compliance, audit |
| Revenue Operations Analyst | Pricing ops, billing, collections, revenue recognition |

Human roles are amplified by the [department agents](agents.md). Every role follows the
[principles](principles.md) and the [review process](review-process.md).
