# DevOps - Metrics

**North-star:** Elite DORA performance - frequent, fast, low-failure, quick-recovery delivery.

**Delivery (DORA)** - deployment frequency, lead time, change failure rate, MTTR

**Reliability** - uptime vs. SLO, error-budget burn, pipeline success rate

**Efficiency** - infrastructure cost, IaC coverage, provisioning time

Metrics are surfaced on the [dashboards](dashboards.md) and feed the
[Continuous Improvement Engine](../orchestration/continuous-improvement-engine.md).
