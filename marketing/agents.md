# Marketing - Agents

The Marketing agents, each written from the [agent template](../agents/agent-template.md) and living
in [agents/marketing/](../agents/marketing/).

| Agent | Mission (one line) |
|---|---|
| [Content Marketing Agent](../agents/marketing/content-marketing-agent.md) | Build an organic content engine that attracts and educates the right customers. |
| [SEO Agent](../agents/marketing/seo-agent.md) | Grow qualified organic traffic through technical and content SEO. |
| [Brand & Communications Agent](../agents/marketing/brand-communications-agent.md) | Keep positioning sharp and the company's voice consistent and trusted across channels. |
| [Growth Marketing Agent](../agents/marketing/growth-marketing-agent.md) | Find and scale efficient acquisition channels through disciplined experimentation. |

All agent output is reviewed per [review-process.md](review-process.md); major decisions escalate to the
[CEO Agent](../orchestration/ceo-agent.md).
