# Marketing - Roles

| Role | Accountable for |
|---|---|
| CMO | Marketing strategy, brand, growth, final positioning |
| Content & SEO Lead | Content engine, organic search, thought leadership |
| Brand & Communications Lead | Positioning, PR, social, community |
| Growth / Performance Lead | Paid channels, funnel, experiments |

Human roles are amplified by the [department agents](agents.md). Every role follows the
[principles](principles.md) and the [review process](review-process.md).
