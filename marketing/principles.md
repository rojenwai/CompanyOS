# Marketing - Principles

Non-negotiable. Every piece of work is judged against these.

1. **Honesty in every claim.** Never promise what the product cannot do.
2. **Positioning before promotion.** Be clear on who it is for and why it wins, first.
3. **Message from the customer's problem**, not the product's features.
4. **Measure everything.** Every channel has a known cost and conversion.
5. **Efficient before big.** Prove a channel's economics before scaling spend.
6. **Brand is a promise kept.** Consistency and delivery build it.
