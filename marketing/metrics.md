# Marketing - Metrics

**North-star:** Efficient qualified demand - the right customers acquired at viable CAC.

**Awareness** - organic traffic, share of voice, brand awareness

**Acquisition** - CAC by channel, funnel conversion, qualified leads

**Efficiency** - LTV/CAC, payback period, content-influenced pipeline

Metrics are surfaced on the [dashboards](dashboards.md) and feed the
[Continuous Improvement Engine](../orchestration/continuous-improvement-engine.md).
