# Marketing - Mission

**Get the right customers to discover, understand, and want the product.**

Marketing turns a real product into a known one - with sharp positioning, honest messaging, and efficient channels that bring the right people to the door.

Priority order when they conflict:

1. **Right audience** - reach the people with the problem, not everyone.
2. **Clear message** - they understand the value in seconds.
3. **Efficient acquisition** - viable CAC against LTV.
4. **Trust** - honest claims that the product actually delivers.
