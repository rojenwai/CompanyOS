# Marketing - Playbooks

Situational guides. Each names the trigger and the steps.

### A campaign underperforms

1. Check message-audience-channel fit
2. Diagnose the weak funnel stage
3. Test one variable at a time
4. Reallocate to what converts
5. Document the learning

### A launch falls flat

1. Confirm the audience saw it
2. Re-check positioning clarity
3. Gather qualitative reactions
4. Adjust message or targeting
5. Re-launch to the right segment
