# Marketing - Decision Frameworks

How decisions are made here. All decisions follow the company
[decision framework](../governance/decision-framework.md); these are the department-specific ones.

- **Scale vs. kill a channel** - Scale only channels with viable, proven CAC/LTV; kill the rest fast.
- **Brand vs. performance spend** - Balance long-term brand with measurable performance; never at the cost of honesty.
- **Build audience vs. buy attention** - Invest in owned audience (content, community) alongside paid to reduce long-run CAC.

Reversible decisions are made fast and revisited; irreversible ones require review and are recorded in
[decision memory](../memory/decision-memory.md).
