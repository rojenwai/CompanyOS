# Review Process

> How work produced in this department is reviewed before it is accepted. Every significant output
> is reviewed by at least one other role/agent — the reviewer never assumes the author is correct.

## What gets reviewed

- <artifact type> → reviewed by <role/agent> against <checklist>.

## Review checklist

- [ ] Meets [standards.md](standards.md)
- [ ] Passes [quality-gates.md](quality-gates.md)
- [ ] Risks identified and mitigated
- [ ] Evidence cited where claims are made

## Outcomes

Approve · Approve with changes · Reject with reasons · Escalate (see [decision-frameworks.md](decision-frameworks.md)).
