# Hardware - Metrics

**North-star:** Safe, reliable products in the field with low defect and RMA rates.

**Quality** - field failure rate, RMA rate, first-pass yield

**Safety** - safety test coverage, safety incidents

**Updates** - OTA success rate, fleet firmware freshness

Metrics are surfaced on the [dashboards](dashboards.md) and feed the
[Continuous Improvement Engine](../orchestration/continuous-improvement-engine.md).
