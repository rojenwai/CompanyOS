# People & HR - Workflows

The core repeatable processes this department runs.

### Hire for a role

1. Approve the role and headcount with [finance](../finance/README.md)
2. Write the scorecard
3. Source and screen
4. Run structured interviews
5. Debrief and decide on evidence
6. Offer and close
7. Hand to onboarding

### Onboard a new hire

1. Provision access and equipment before day 1
2. Deliver the role onboarding path
3. Assign a buddy and first task
4. Check in at days 7 and 30
5. Confirm time-to-productivity
