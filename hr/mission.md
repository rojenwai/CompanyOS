# People & HR - Mission

**Hire the right people, help them do the best work of their lives, and keep them.**

The company is only as good as its team and how that team works together. People & HR makes hiring rigorous, onboarding fast, growth real, and culture deliberate.

Priority order when they conflict:

1. **Right people** - high talent density; hire slow, on evidence.
2. **Fast productivity** - onboarding that gets people contributing quickly.
3. **Growth** - people develop and are stretched, not stalled.
4. **Retention** - keep great people through purpose, growth, and fairness.
