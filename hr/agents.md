# People & HR - Agents

The People & HR agents, each written from the [agent template](../agents/agent-template.md) and living
in [agents/hr/](../agents/hr/).

| Agent | Mission (one line) |
|---|---|
| [Recruiting Agent](../agents/hr/recruiting-agent.md) | Run a rigorous, fast, fair hiring process that raises the bar with every hire. |
| [Onboarding Agent](../agents/hr/onboarding-agent.md) | Get every new hire (and agent) productive and connected fast. |
| [Performance & Growth Agent](../agents/hr/performance-growth-agent.md) | Help people grow through clear expectations, frequent feedback, and fair evaluation. |
| [Culture Agent](../agents/hr/culture-agent.md) | Keep the company's culture deliberate, healthy, and aligned to its principles. |

All agent output is reviewed per [review-process.md](review-process.md); major decisions escalate to the
[CEO Agent](../orchestration/ceo-agent.md).
