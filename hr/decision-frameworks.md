# People & HR - Decision Frameworks

How decisions are made here. All decisions follow the company
[decision framework](../governance/decision-framework.md); these are the department-specific ones.

- **Hire vs. no-hire** - Hire only on clear evidence of meeting the bar; when in doubt, no-hire.
- **Build the team vs. augment with agents** - Use agents to amplify people; hire humans for judgment, ownership, and relationships.
- **Promote vs. develop-in-place** - Promote on demonstrated scope; otherwise create stretch to grow into it.

Reversible decisions are made fast and revisited; irreversible ones require review and are recorded in
[decision memory](../memory/decision-memory.md).
