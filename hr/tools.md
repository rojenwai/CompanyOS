# People & HR - Tools

Tooling is a means, not an end. Categories used here:

| Category | Examples |
|---|---|
| Recruiting | ATS, sourcing, structured-interview kits |
| People ops | HRIS, onboarding workflows |
| Development | Performance/goal and feedback platforms |
| Culture | Engagement surveys, sentiment analysis |

Tool choices follow the [build vs. buy framework](../engineering/decision-frameworks.md).
