# People & HR - Standard Operating Procedures

### SOP-01 - Run a structured interview loop

1. Confirm the scorecard
2. Assign interviewers to competencies
3. Interview with consistent questions
4. Collect written, evidence-based feedback
5. Debrief and decide

### SOP-02 - Design an onboarding path

1. List the role's first-30-day outcomes
2. Sequence learning and access
3. Define early wins and a buddy
4. Add check-ins
5. Measure and iterate
