# Product Principles

Every feature should:

- Reduce friction
- Save time
- Reduce cost
- Increase reliability
- Improve usability
- Create delight
- Strengthen retention
- Support long-term strategy

## Operating stance

- **Discovery precedes development** — validate before building ([discovery](discovery.md)).
- **Evidence over opinion** — decisions are grounded in research and [analytics](analytics.md).
- **The smallest useful product** — an [MVP](../agents/product/mvp-planning-agent.md) solves one critical problem exceptionally well, not many problems poorly.
- **Outcome over output** — success is customer and business outcomes, not shipped feature count ([metrics](metrics.md)).

These specialize the company [core principles](../company/principles.md).
