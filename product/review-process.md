# Product Review Process

Every feature is reviewed for: customer value · business impact · technical feasibility · UX quality ·
accessibility · security · performance · maintainability.

## Reviews

- **PRD review** — PM + engineering + design + security sign off before build.
- **Design review** — against the [design system](../design/README.md) and [accessibility](../design/accessibility.md).
- **Pre-launch review** — the [launch checklist](quality-gates.md) gate.
- **Post-launch review** — did it move its success metric? ([metrics](metrics.md))

## Outcomes

Approve · Approve with changes · Reject with reasons · Escalate to the [CPO](organization.md) or
[CEO Agent](../orchestration/ceo-agent.md). Reviewers never rubber-stamp.
