# Product Decision Frameworks — Prioritization

How product ranks work consistently, by evidence rather than opinion. Owned by the
[Feature Prioritization Agent](../agents/product/feature-prioritization-agent.md).

## What we evaluate

Business value · customer impact · revenue · risk · technical complexity · cost · strategic
alignment · dependencies.

## RICE

**Reach × Impact × Confidence ÷ Effort.** Best for comparing many features on a common scale.

## MoSCoW

**Must have · Should have · Could have · Won't have (now).** Best for scoping a release.

## Kano

**Basic features · Performance features · Delighters.** Best for balancing table-stakes against
differentiation.

## Choosing

- Comparing a large backlog → **RICE**.
- Scoping a specific release → **MoSCoW**.
- Deciding where to invest for differentiation → **Kano**.

Reversible calls are made by the PM; irreversible/strategic bets go through the company
[decision framework](../governance/decision-framework.md) and [approval](../orchestration/approval-engine.md).
