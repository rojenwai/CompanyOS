# Mission

The product exists to solve customer problems.

**Not** to demonstrate technology. **Not** to maximize feature count. **Not** to copy competitors.

Every feature must increase customer value.

## What this means

- We start from a validated problem and a real customer, established in [discovery](discovery.md).
- We ship the smallest thing that solves the problem exceptionally well ([MVP](../agents/product/mvp-planning-agent.md)).
- We measure whether it worked and iterate ([metrics](metrics.md), [analytics](analytics.md)).

This specializes the company [mission](../company/mission.md) for the product domain.
