# Product Playbooks

## Playbook: no product-market fit yet
**When:** retention is weak and referrals aren't happening.
1. Return to [discovery](discovery.md); re-validate the problem and persona. 2. Narrow to the single most acute job. 3. Ship a sharper [MVP](../agents/product/mvp-planning-agent.md). 4. Re-measure the [PMF indicators](metrics.md).

## Playbook: feature not adopted
**When:** a shipped feature's [success metric](metrics.md) is flat.
1. Check the [journey](customer-journey.md) for discoverability/friction. 2. Interview non-adopters. 3. Decide: improve, reposition, or [deprecate](../post-launch/deprecation-policy.md).

## Playbook: conflicting stakeholder priorities
1. Re-score with a shared [framework](decision-frameworks.md). 2. Tie to the [north-star metric](metrics.md). 3. Escalate unresolved strategic conflicts to the [CEO Agent](../orchestration/ceo-agent.md).
