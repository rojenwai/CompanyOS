# Customer Journey

Map every interaction a customer has with the product, end to end, to find and remove friction.

## Stages

Awareness → Interest → Evaluation → Purchase → Onboarding → Activation → Retention → Expansion → Advocacy.

## For each stage capture

- The customer's **goal** at that moment.
- Their **actions** and **touchpoints**.
- **Friction / pain** and **emotions**.
- The **opportunity** to improve.

## Usage

Journey maps drive [prioritization](decision-frameworks.md) (fix the highest-friction, highest-value
moments first) and feed the [product metrics](metrics.md) that track activation, retention, and
expansion. Owned by the [Customer Journey Agent](../agents/product/customer-journey-agent.md).
