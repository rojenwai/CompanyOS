# Operations - Workflows

The core repeatable processes this department runs.

### Run the operating cadence

1. Daily: unblock and watch health
2. Weekly: status and metrics review
3. Per sprint: plan, review, retro
4. Monthly: business review
5. Quarterly: strategy and roadmap review

### Coordinate a cross-team initiative

1. Define the goal, owners, and plan
2. Map dependencies
3. Track status in one place
4. Surface and drive blockers
5. Report and close out with learnings
