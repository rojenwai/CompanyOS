# Operations - Roles

| Role | Accountable for |
|---|---|
| COO | Operational strategy, cadences, final process decisions |
| Program / Project Manager | Planning, coordination, delivery, risk |
| Process & Tools Owner | Internal processes, systems, automation |
| Vendor & Asset Manager | Vendors, licenses, equipment, access lifecycle |

Human roles are amplified by the [department agents](agents.md). Every role follows the
[principles](principles.md) and the [review process](review-process.md).
