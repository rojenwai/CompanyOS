# Operations - Standards

Testable standards, not aspirations. These extend the company-wide
[standards](../standards/README.md).

1. Every initiative has an owner, a plan, and a single status source.
2. Decisions are recorded in [decision memory](../memory/decision-memory.md).
3. Cadences (daily to quarterly) run on schedule.
4. Processes are the lightest that reliably produce the outcome.
5. Vendor access is de-provisioned promptly at off-boarding.
