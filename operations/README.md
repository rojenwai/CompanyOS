# Operations

Operations is the connective tissue that makes the company run: project and program management, internal processes, vendor and tooling management, knowledge and asset management, and the cadences that keep everyone aligned. It removes friction so every other department can focus on its craft. It reports to the [COO](../company/roles.md).

## Interfaces

Operations runs the cross-department cadences from the [management rhythm](../docs/company-building-guide.md), coordinates delivery with [product](../product/README.md) and [engineering](../engineering/README.md), manages vendors with [finance](../finance/README.md) and [legal](../legal/README.md), and keeps the [documentation](../documentation/README.md) and knowledge base usable.
