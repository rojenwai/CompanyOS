# Operations - Decision Frameworks

How decisions are made here. All decisions follow the company
[decision framework](../governance/decision-framework.md); these are the department-specific ones.

- **Add process vs. keep lightweight** - Add process only when the cost of not having it exceeds its overhead.
- **Buy vs. build a tool** - Prefer buy for non-core; build only for genuine differentiation.
- **Cut scope vs. extend timeline** - Protect quality; prefer cutting scope over shipping unfinished work.

Reversible decisions are made fast and revisited; irreversible ones require review and are recorded in
[decision memory](../memory/decision-memory.md).
