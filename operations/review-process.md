# Operations - Review Process

Operational work is reviewed for clarity (are owners, plans, and status clear?), leverage (is the process worth its overhead?), and follow-through (are decisions recorded and blockers resolved?). The COO reviews cross-team process changes.

Reviews are independent - the [Reviewer](../orchestration/reviewer.md) never assumes the author is
correct. Security-sensitive work also requires the
[Security Reviewer](../orchestration/security-reviewer.md).
