# Example - Weekly Operating Cadence

> Worked example - illustrative, not a live record.

| Day | Ritual | Output |
|---|---|---|
| Mon | Leadership sync (30m) | Priorities and blockers for the week |
| Daily | Team standups (async) | Status, blockers surfaced |
| Wed | Metrics review (30m) | North-star and department metrics read |
| Fri | Demo + retro | Shipped work shown; one improvement adopted |

**Rule:** every blocker raised in standup has an owner and a next step by end of day.
