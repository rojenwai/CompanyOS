# Operations - Agents

The Operations agents, each written from the [agent template](../agents/agent-template.md) and living
in [agents/operations/](../agents/operations/).

| Agent | Mission (one line) |
|---|---|
| [Project Management Agent](../agents/operations/project-management-agent.md) | Keep initiatives planned, coordinated, and unblocked to on-time delivery. |
| [Process Optimization Agent](../agents/operations/process-optimization-agent.md) | Design the lightest processes that reliably produce the outcome, and automate the routine. |
| [Vendor Management Agent](../agents/operations/vendor-management-agent.md) | Get the right tools and services at the right price and terms, and keep them managed. |
| [Knowledge Management Agent](../agents/operations/knowledge-management-agent.md) | Keep the company's knowledge and assets findable, current, and owned. |

All agent output is reviewed per [review-process.md](review-process.md); major decisions escalate to the
[CEO Agent](../orchestration/ceo-agent.md).
