# Operations - Principles

Non-negotiable. Every piece of work is judged against these.

1. **Process serves people.** The lightest process that produces the outcome wins.
2. **Single source of truth.** One place for status, decisions, and plans.
3. **Surface blockers early.** Silent blockers are the enemy.
4. **Decisions are recorded.** In [decision memory](../memory/decision-memory.md), not lost in chat.
5. **Cadence over chaos.** Predictable rhythms beat heroics.
6. **Automate the routine.** Free humans for judgment.
