# SaaS - Added / Activated

Beyond the core [agents](../../agents/README.md), this kit leans on these departments and agents.
Write any new agents from the [agent template](../../agents/agent-template.md).

## Departments to activate first

- [customer-success/](../../customer-success/README.md) - retention and NRR are the business
- [finance/](../../finance/README.md) - subscription billing, MRR/ARR, unit economics
- [devops/](../../devops/README.md) - uptime SLAs and multi-tenant reliability
- [data/](../../data/README.md) - product analytics for activation and churn

## Emphasis

Emphasize the [Revenue & Billing Agent](../../agents/finance/revenue-billing-agent.md), [Customer Health Agent](../../agents/customer-success/customer-health-agent.md), and [Site Reliability Agent](../../agents/devops/site-reliability-agent.md).
