# SaaS - Added / Activated

Beyond the core [agents](../../ai/agents/README.md), this kit leans on these departments and agents.
Write any new agents from the [agent template](../../ai/agents/agent-template.md).

## Departments to activate first

- [customer-success/](../../handbook/departments/customer-success/README.md) - retention and NRR are the business
- [finance/](../../handbook/departments/finance/README.md) - subscription billing, MRR/ARR, unit economics
- [devops/](../../handbook/departments/devops/README.md) - uptime SLAs and multi-tenant reliability
- [data/](../../handbook/departments/data/README.md) - product analytics for activation and churn

## Emphasis

Emphasize the [Revenue & Billing Agent](../../ai/agents/finance/revenue-billing-agent.md), [Customer Health Agent](../../ai/agents/customer-success/customer-health-agent.md), and [Site Reliability Agent](../../ai/agents/devops/site-reliability-agent.md).
