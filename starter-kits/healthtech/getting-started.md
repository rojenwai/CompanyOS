# Health Tech - Getting Started

1. Adopt the core; validate the clinical problem **with clinicians**, not just buyers.
2. Determine regulatory classification with [legal](../../handbook/departments/legal/README.md) before building.
3. Design for PHI minimisation and auditability from the first schema.
4. Plan the evidence: what study proves this works, and who accepts it?
5. Build under a quality system with continuous regulatory documentation.
6. Launch with post-market surveillance and adverse-event reporting in place.

> Everything not listed here comes from core Company OS - this kit only adds the domain-specific deltas.
