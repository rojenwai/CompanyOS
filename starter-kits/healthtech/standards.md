# Health Tech - Added Standards

These extend the core [standards](../../handbook/standards/README.md) with what this company type specifically requires.

1. **Classify the product before building it.** Is it a regulated medical device (e.g. SaMD)? The answer changes everything - [legal](../../handbook/departments/legal/README.md) owns this gate.
2. **Patient safety outranks every other priority**, including speed and revenue.
3. **Protect PHI by default:** minimum-necessary collection, encryption, strict access control, and complete audit trails ([security](../../handbook/departments/security/README.md)).
4. **No clinical claim without clinical evidence.** Efficacy is demonstrated, not asserted ([research ethics](../../handbook/departments/research/research-ethics.md)).
5. **Keep a human in the loop for clinical decisions** ([approval engine](../../ai/orchestration/approval-engine.md)); AI supports clinicians, it does not replace their judgement.
6. **Maintain regulatory and quality documentation continuously**, not before an audit ([documentation](../../handbook/departments/documentation/README.md)).
