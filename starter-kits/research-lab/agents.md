# Research Lab - Added / Activated

Beyond the core [agents](../../ai/agents/README.md), this kit leans on these departments and agents.
Write any new agents from the [agent template](../../ai/agents/agent-template.md).

## Departments to activate first

- [research/](../../handbook/departments/research/README.md) - the core function: rigorous discovery
- [ai/](../../handbook/departments/ai-engineering/README.md) - experimentation, evaluation, novel models
- [legal/](../../handbook/departments/legal/README.md) - IP, patents, licensing, grants
- [documentation/](../../handbook/departments/documentation/README.md) - papers, reproducible write-ups

## Emphasis

Emphasize the research agents ([Problem Discovery](../../ai/agents/research/problem-discovery-agent.md), [Opportunity Ranking](../../ai/agents/research/opportunity-ranking-agent.md)), the AI [Evaluation Agent](../../ai/agents/ai-engineering/ai-evaluation-agent.md), and the [IP & Patent Agent](../../ai/agents/legal/ip-patent-agent.md).
