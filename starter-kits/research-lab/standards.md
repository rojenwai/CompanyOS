# Research Lab - Added Standards

These extend the core [standards](../../handbook/standards/README.md) with what this company type specifically requires.

1. Every result is reproducible from cited sources and recorded method ([research ethics](../../handbook/departments/research/research-ethics.md)).
2. Facts, assumptions, and predictions are always distinguished; no fabricated evidence.
3. IP is protected deliberately (patent vs. trade secret) before disclosure ([legal](../../handbook/departments/legal/README.md)).
4. Findings are peer-reviewed internally before external claims ([research review](../../handbook/departments/research/review-process.md)).
