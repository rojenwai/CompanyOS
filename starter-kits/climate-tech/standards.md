# Climate Tech - Added Standards

These extend the core [standards](../../handbook/standards/README.md) with what this company type specifically requires.

1. **Assess and report [TRL](../../handbook/departments/research/technology-readiness.md) honestly.** A TRL-3 result is a research bet, not a product - never inflate it to investors or customers.
2. **Fund the crossing of the valley of death (TRL 4-6) deliberately** - it is a strategic decision, not an afterthought.
3. **Every impact claim is measured and verified (MRV) with real data**, using a stated methodology and baseline.
4. **No greenwashing.** Claims are verifiable, bounded, and distinguish measured results from projections ([research ethics](../../handbook/departments/research/research-ethics.md)).
5. **Account for full lifecycle impact**, not just the operating phase.
6. **Safety and environmental compliance are release gates** for any physical system ([hardware quality gates](../../handbook/departments/hardware/quality-gates.md)).
