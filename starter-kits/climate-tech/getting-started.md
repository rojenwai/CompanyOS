# Climate Tech - Getting Started

1. Adopt the core; frame the climate problem from [first principles](../../handbook/departments/research/first-principles.md).
2. Assess your honest starting [TRL](../../handbook/departments/research/technology-readiness.md) and name the evidence that advances each level.
3. Plan and fund the TRL 4-6 crossing explicitly (grants, pilots, partners).
4. Design MRV from day one - if you cannot measure the impact, you cannot claim it.
5. Activate [hardware](../../handbook/departments/hardware/README.md) with safety as a hard gate.
6. Deploy, verify impact with real data, then scale.

> Everything not listed here comes from core Company OS - this kit only adds the domain-specific deltas.
