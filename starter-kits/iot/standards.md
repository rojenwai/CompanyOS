# IoT Platform - Added Standards

These extend the core [standards](../../handbook/standards/README.md) with what this company type specifically requires.

1. Every device has a unique identity and authenticated, encrypted connectivity ([security](../../handbook/departments/security/README.md)).
2. Telemetry pipelines are reliable and tested at fleet scale ([data](../../handbook/departments/data/README.md)).
3. Fleet OTA is staged (canary) with rollback; devices degrade gracefully offline.
4. Data retention and privacy are governed ([data governance](../../ai/agents/data/data-governance-agent.md)).
