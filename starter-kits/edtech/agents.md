# Ed Tech - Added / Activated

Beyond the core [agents](../../ai/agents/README.md), this kit leans on these departments and agents.
Write any new agents from the [agent template](../../ai/agents/agent-template.md).

## Departments to activate first

- [research/](../../handbook/departments/research/README.md) - learning science and efficacy studies
- [design/](../../handbook/departments/design/README.md) - accessibility and age-appropriate design
- [legal/](../../handbook/departments/legal/README.md) - student-data privacy (FERPA/COPPA/GDPR)
- [sales/](../../handbook/departments/sales/README.md) + [customer-success/](../../handbook/departments/customer-success/README.md) - institutional buying and rollout

## Emphasis

Emphasize the research agents for efficacy evidence, the [Accessibility Agent](../../ai/agents/design/accessibility-agent.md), the [Privacy & Terms Agent](../../ai/agents/legal/privacy-terms-agent.md), and the [Sales Engineer Agent](../../ai/agents/sales/sales-engineer-agent.md) for institutional procurement.
