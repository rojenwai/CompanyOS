# Ed Tech - Added Standards

These extend the core [standards](../../handbook/standards/README.md) with what this company type specifically requires.

1. **Prove learning outcomes with evidence.** Engagement is not learning; efficacy is measured against a baseline ([research framework](../../handbook/departments/research/research-framework.md)).
2. **No unsupported efficacy claims** in marketing or sales ([research ethics](../../handbook/departments/research/research-ethics.md), [marketing standards](../../handbook/departments/marketing/standards.md)).
3. **Accessibility is non-negotiable** - meet WCAG; learners with disabilities are first-class users ([accessibility](../../handbook/departments/design/accessibility.md)).
4. **Minimise and protect student data**, especially for minors; comply with FERPA/COPPA/GDPR as applicable ([legal](../../handbook/departments/legal/README.md)).
5. **Age-appropriate design; no dark patterns and no engagement-maximising mechanics aimed at children** ([marketing psychology](../../handbook/departments/marketing/psychology.md)).
6. **Educators are stakeholders, not obstacles** - design for the classroom reality, not the demo.
