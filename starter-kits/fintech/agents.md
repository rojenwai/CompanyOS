# Fintech - Added / Activated

Beyond the core [agents](../../ai/agents/README.md), this kit leans on these departments and agents.
Write any new agents from the [agent template](../../ai/agents/agent-template.md).

## Departments to activate first

- [legal/](../../handbook/departments/legal/README.md) - licensing, regulatory permissions, partner banks
- [security/](../../handbook/departments/security/README.md) - PCI-DSS, encryption, access, audit trails
- [finance/](../../handbook/departments/finance/README.md) - ledgers, reconciliation, treasury
- [data/](../../handbook/departments/data/README.md) - fraud, risk models, transaction monitoring

## Emphasis

Emphasize the [Compliance Agent](../../ai/agents/legal/compliance-agent.md), [Compliance & Privacy Agent](../../ai/agents/security/compliance-privacy-agent.md), [Accounting Agent](../../ai/agents/finance/accounting-agent.md), and [Data Scientist Agent](../../ai/agents/data/data-scientist-agent.md) for fraud and risk.
