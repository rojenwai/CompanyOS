# Fintech - Getting Started

1. Adopt the core; validate the financial problem and the segment.
2. Scope regulation **first** with [legal](../../handbook/departments/legal/README.md) - license, partner bank, or exemption.
3. Design the double-entry ledger and idempotent money movement before any UI.
4. Build KYC/AML and fraud controls into the product from day one.
5. Pass a full [security review](../../handbook/templates/documents/security-review.md) and compliance audit.
6. Launch narrowly; reconcile daily and monitor fraud ([post-launch](../../handbook/departments/post-launch/README.md)).

> Everything not listed here comes from core Company OS - this kit only adds the domain-specific deltas.
