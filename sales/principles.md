# Sales - Principles

Non-negotiable. Every piece of work is judged against these.

1. **Sell fit, not features.** If the product is wrong for them, say so.
2. **Honesty compounds.** Overselling creates churn and refunds; underselling loses deals - be accurate.
3. **Discover before you pitch.** Understand the problem first.
4. **Value over discount.** Anchor on outcome; discount deliberately, not reflexively.
5. **CRM is the source of truth.** Every interaction and commitment is logged.
6. **Hand off cleanly.** Set [customer-success](../customer-success/README.md) up to win.
