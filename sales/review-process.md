# Sales - Review Process

Deals are reviewed for fit (is this a customer we can make successful?), honesty (are expectations accurate?), and terms (within policy, or approved?). The Revenue Lead reviews the forecast; legal and finance review non-standard terms.

Reviews are independent - the [Reviewer](../orchestration/reviewer.md) never assumes the author is
correct. Security-sensitive work also requires the
[Security Reviewer](../orchestration/security-reviewer.md).
