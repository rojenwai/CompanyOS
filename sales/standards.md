# Sales - Standards

Testable standards, not aspirations. These extend the company-wide
[standards](../standards/README.md).

1. Never oversell; expectations must match what the product delivers.
2. Every interaction and commitment is logged in the CRM.
3. Pricing and discounts stay within policy or are approved.
4. Feature and delivery commitments require product/engineering sign-off.
5. Every closed deal gets a complete customer-success handoff.
