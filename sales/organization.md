# Sales - Organization

## Reporting

Revenue leadership (the [CMO](../company/roles.md) or a dedicated sales leader), with the CEO closing early strategic deals.

```
Revenue lead
|-- Prospecting / SDR   - qualify inbound and outbound
|-- Account Executives  - discovery, solution, close
`-- Sales Engineering   - technical fit, proofs, security reviews
```

## Interfaces

| Works with | On |
|---|---|
| [Marketing](../marketing/README.md) | Qualified demand handoff |
| [Product](../product/README.md) | Value, roadmap, feasibility of asks |
| [Legal](../legal/README.md) / [Finance](../finance/README.md) | Contracts, pricing, terms |
| [Customer Success](../customer-success/README.md) | Post-sale handoff |

## Where this plugs into the kernel

Work is decomposed and routed by the [coordinator](../orchestration/coordinator.md), reviewed at the
[quality gates](quality-gates.md), and its lasting knowledge persists to
[memory](../memory/README.md). Major decisions require cross-functional review before
[CEO Agent](../orchestration/ceo-agent.md) approval.
