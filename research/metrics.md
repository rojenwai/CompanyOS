# Research - Metrics

**North-star:** The share of shipped bets that were correctly identified and ranked in advance.

**Discovery** - problems validated, evidence quality score, signal-to-validation time

**Ranking** - top-bet hit rate, scoring consistency, portfolio value realized

**Freshness** - landscape coverage, source freshness, watch latency

Metrics are surfaced on the [dashboards](dashboards.md) and feed the
[Continuous Improvement Engine](../orchestration/continuous-improvement-engine.md).
