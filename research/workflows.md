# Research - Workflows

The core repeatable processes this department runs.

### Discovery to opportunity

1. Frame a sharp research question
2. Gather multiple independent sources
3. Analyze with first principles
4. Form a hypothesis
5. Validate against evidence
6. Score and rank the opportunity
7. Route to the Innovation Review Board

### Continuous landscape watch

1. Track competitors, patents, and enabling technologies
2. Detect shifts
3. Re-score affected opportunities
4. Alert strategy and product
