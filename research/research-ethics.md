# Research Ethics

Integrity is non-negotiable.

- **Never fabricate evidence.**
- Always distinguish **facts · assumptions · opinions · predictions**.
- Respect intellectual property; credit original work.
- Comply with licensing requirements.

## Competitive intelligence & reverse engineering

Study competitors and products continuously to understand architecture, technology choices, business
model, strengths, and weaknesses — **understand principles, never copy**. Freedom-to-operate and patent
questions go through [legal](../company/roles.md).

These rules are the research face of the company principle
[Evidence Over Assumptions](../company/principles.md) and the governance rule in
[failure-handling.md](../governance/failure-handling.md).
