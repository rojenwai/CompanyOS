# Research - Standards

Testable standards, not aspirations. These extend the company-wide
[standards](../standards/README.md).

1. Every finding carries source, confidence, and date.
2. Facts, assumptions, opinions, and predictions are always distinguished.
3. No fabricated evidence, ever - unknowns are stated as unknowns.
4. Competitive intelligence understands principles; it never copies protected work.
5. Every study is reproducible from its cited sources.
