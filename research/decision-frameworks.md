# Research - Decision Frameworks

How decisions are made here. All decisions follow the company
[decision framework](../governance/decision-framework.md); these are the department-specific ones.

- **Pursue vs. defer vs. decline** - Based on the opportunity score, mission fit, and feasibility.
- **Primary vs. secondary research** - Do primary research only when secondary sources cannot answer the question.
- **Innovation Review Board** - Significant new bets require the board's eight-question review ([innovation-framework.md](innovation-framework.md)).

Reversible decisions are made fast and revisited; irreversible ones require review and are recorded in
[decision memory](../memory/decision-memory.md).
