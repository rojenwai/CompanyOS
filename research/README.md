The purpose of research is not to collect information — it is to **discover opportunities others have
overlooked and turn them into defensible products.** Research answers one question:
**"What should this company build next?"**

Research is continuous. Every agent is responsible for discovering new technologies, emerging markets,
customer pain points, competitive threats, scientific breakthroughs, and business opportunities.

|---|---|---|

| H2 | Create adjacent products | 2–5 years |
| H3 | Pursue breakthrough innovations | 5–10+ years |

## Research principles

Research continuously reduces uncertainty; innovation continuously increases value. Every experiment,
success, and failure contributes to a growing body of knowledge that compounds over time — stored in
[research memory](../memory/research-memory.md). Findings are reported using the
[research report template](../templates/documents/research-report.md).
