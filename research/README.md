# Research

Research discovers opportunities others overlook and turns them into defensible products. It answers the company's most important question - *what should we build next?* - with evidence, not opinion. It reports to the [Chief Scientist](../company/roles.md) and feeds [strategy](../strategy/README.md) and [product](../product/README.md).

## Interfaces

Research supplies validated problems and opportunity scores to [strategy](../strategy/startup-lifecycle.md) and [product discovery](../product/discovery.md); technical findings go to [engineering](../engineering/README.md) and [ai](../ai/README.md); all findings persist in [research memory](../memory/research-memory.md) and the [knowledge base](../memory/knowledge-base.md).
