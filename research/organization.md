# Research - Organization

## Reporting

The [Chief Scientist](../company/roles.md) owns technology research, scientific validation, and academic and patent research.

```
Chief Scientist
|-- Problem & Market Research  - problems, demand, market sizing
|-- Competitive & Technology   - competitors, patents, enabling tech
`-- Scientific / Academic      - literature, validation, feasibility
```

## Interfaces

| Works with | On |
|---|---|
| [Strategy](../strategy/README.md) | Opportunities, market sizing, competitive intelligence |
| [Product](../product/README.md) | Validated problems, personas, discovery evidence |
| [AI](../ai/README.md) / [Engineering](../engineering/README.md) | Technical feasibility, enabling technologies |
| [Governance](../governance/README.md) | Innovation Review Board decisions |

## Where this plugs into the kernel

Work is decomposed and routed by the [coordinator](../orchestration/coordinator.md), reviewed at the
[quality gates](quality-gates.md), and its lasting knowledge persists to
[memory](../memory/README.md). Major decisions require cross-functional review before
[CEO Agent](../orchestration/ceo-agent.md) approval.
