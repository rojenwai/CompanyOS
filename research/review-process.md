# Research - Review Process

Research output is reviewed for evidentiary soundness (are claims sourced and correctly qualified?) and significance (does this matter?). Opportunity scores are reviewed by the Innovation Review Board before they influence the roadmap.

Reviews are independent - the [Reviewer](../orchestration/reviewer.md) never assumes the author is
correct. Security-sensitive work also requires the
[Security Reviewer](../orchestration/security-reviewer.md).
