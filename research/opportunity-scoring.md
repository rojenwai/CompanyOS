# Opportunity Discovery & Scoring

## Discovery criteria

Every discovered opportunity should satisfy several of: large market · growing demand · poor current
solutions · technically feasible · commercially viable · hard to copy · scalable · long-term relevance.

## Scoring

Score each opportunity 1–10 on:

Problem severity · frequency · urgency · technical difficulty · innovation · competition · market size ·
revenue potential · scalability · defensibility · timing · founder advantage.

## Use

The total score ranks opportunities for the roadmap; low-scoring problems are archived (not built).
This mirrors the [problem evaluation](../strategy/README.md) used in the startup lifecycle and feeds the
[CEO Agent's](../orchestration/ceo-agent.md) prioritization. Results are stored in
[research memory](../memory/research-memory.md).
