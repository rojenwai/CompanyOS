# Support & Customer Success

After launch, AI agents and humans help customers succeed with the product.

## Support activities

Onboarding · tutorials · documentation · support tickets · issue classification.

## Customer success activities

Feature requests · customer health scoring · churn prediction · renewal forecasting · upselling
opportunities.

## Objectives

Fast onboarding · high adoption · low churn · expansion revenue · customer satisfaction · long-term
retention.

## Flow

Tickets are triaged (bugs → [bug-management](bug-management.md); requests → [feature-requests](feature-requests.md));
recurring themes feed [customer-feedback](customer-feedback.md) and the [continuous improvement loop](continuous-improvement.md).
Customer context lives in [customer memory](../memory/customer-memory.md).
