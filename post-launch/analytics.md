# Post-Launch Analytics

After launch, analytics tell us whether the product is delivering value and where it is losing it.

## What we track

Feature adoption · retention · churn · engagement · conversion · customer usage patterns ·
business metrics · infrastructure cost per customer · AI performance and cost.

## Use

- Confirm whether a launched feature moved its [success metric](../product/metrics.md).
- Detect silent degradation (rising churn, falling adoption) that monitoring alone misses.
- Feed the [continuous improvement loop](continuous-improvement.md) and [product evolution](product-evolution.md).

Product-facing analysis is shared with [product analytics](../product/analytics.md); results are stored
in [research memory](../memory/research-memory.md).
