# Mission

Keep every shipped product reliable, secure, and continuously improving throughout its entire life —
so that the value delivered at launch compounds rather than decays.

## What success looks like

- Products stay within their [SLA](sla.md) with few, well-handled [incidents](incident-response.md).
- Debt, dependencies, and drift are managed before they become failures.
- Customer problems are resolved fast, and their feedback drives the [roadmap](../product/roadmap.md).
- Products are retired responsibly, never abandoned.

## What this department does *not* own

Initial product definition ([product](../product/README.md)) and initial build ([engineering](../engineering/README.md)) —
post-launch takes over at [launch](../product/quality-gates.md) and owns everything after.
