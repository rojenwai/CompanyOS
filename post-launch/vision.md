# Vision

Self-maintaining products: systems that [maintenance agents](agents.md) keep healthy — patched,
performant, and documented — with minimal human intervention, escalating to people only for judgment
and irreversible decisions.

## 3-horizon view

| Horizon | Timeframe | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| H1 | 0–2 years | Reliable monitoring, incident response, and support; debt under control |
| H2 | 2–5 years | Largely automated maintenance (dependencies, patches, health) with human oversight |
| H3 | 5–10+ years | Products that self-heal and self-report, evolving via the [continuous improvement loop](continuous-improvement.md) |
