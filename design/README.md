# Design

Design makes the product usable, accessible, coherent, and trustworthy. It is not decoration - it is
how customers experience everything engineering and product build. Design owns the research into user
behavior, the interaction and visual language, the design system, and the accessibility bar. It reports
to the [Chief Designer](../company/roles.md) and works in lockstep with [product](../product/README.md)
and [engineering](../engineering/README.md).

## Interfaces

Design receives problems and requirements from [product](../product/requirements.md), hands specs and
assets to [engineering](../engineering/README.md), and its work is gated by
[accessibility](accessibility.md) and the [design system](design-system.md). Research findings flow into
[research memory](../memory/research-memory.md).
