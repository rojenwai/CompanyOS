# Design - Decision Frameworks

How decisions are made here. All decisions follow the company
[decision framework](../governance/decision-framework.md); these are the department-specific ones.

- **Reuse vs. new component** - Reuse an existing design-system component unless the need is genuinely new.
- **Custom vs. platform pattern** - Prefer familiar platform patterns; be novel only where it clearly helps the user.
- **Ship vs. iterate on usability findings** - Block on critical usability/accessibility failures; iterate on minor polish.

Reversible decisions are made fast and revisited; irreversible ones require review and are recorded in
[decision memory](../memory/decision-memory.md).
