# UX Research

Study users to ground design in reality, not assumption.

## Study

Behavior · needs · pain points · mental models · accessibility.

## Methods

Interviews · observation · surveys · [usability testing](usability-testing.md) · prototype validation.

## Output

Findings feed [product discovery](../product/discovery.md), [personas](../product/user-personas.md),
and the [customer journey](../product/customer-journey.md). Evidence is stored in
[research memory](../memory/research-memory.md).
