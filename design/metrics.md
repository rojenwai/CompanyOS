# Design - Metrics

**North-star:** Task success - users complete their goals quickly, accessibly, and without confusion.

**Usability** - task completion rate, time on task, error rate, satisfaction (SUS)

**System** - design-system adoption, token drift, component reuse

**Accessibility** - WCAG conformance, critical issues caught pre-release

Metrics are surfaced on the [dashboards](dashboards.md) and feed the
[Continuous Improvement Engine](../orchestration/continuous-improvement-engine.md).
