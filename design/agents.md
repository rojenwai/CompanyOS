# Design - Agents

The Design Division agents, each written from the [agent template](../agents/agent-template.md) and
living in [agents/design/](../agents/design/).

| Agent | Mission (one line) |
|---|---|
| [UX Research Agent](../agents/design/ux-research-agent.md) | Study users and surface behavior, needs, and pain points as evidence |
| [UI Designer Agent](../agents/design/ui-designer-agent.md) | Produce accessible, on-system visual designs from requirements |
| [Interaction Designer Agent](../agents/design/interaction-designer-agent.md) | Design flows, states, feedback, and motion |
| [Design System Agent](../agents/design/design-system-agent.md) | Maintain components, tokens, and accessibility of the design system |
| [Accessibility Agent](../agents/design/accessibility-agent.md) | Audit every surface against WCAG and drive remediation |

These agents collaborate with the [Product Manager](../agents/product/product-manager.md) and hand off to
[frontend engineering](../agents/engineering/frontend-engineer.md). All design output is reviewed per
[review-process.md](review-process.md).
