# Definition of Ready

A task may only enter development when it is **ready**. This prevents half-specified work from
consuming engineering capacity.

A task is ready when it contains:

- **Requirements** — what the system must do.
- **Acceptance Criteria** — specific, testable conditions for completion.
- **Dependencies** — what it needs and what needs it.
- **Design** — the intended approach / UI where applicable.
- **Priority** — its rank against other work.
- **Estimate** — a sized effort.

An unready task is returned to [product](../product/README.md) or the author rather than started.
The completion counterpart is the [Definition of Done](quality-gates.md).
